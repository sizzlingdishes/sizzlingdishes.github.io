MASALA PAPAD BRUSCHETTA

Nov 7th, 2016 3:00 pm

Bruschetta is a fresh, simple and delicious Italian appetizer. This recipe is prepared with an Indian twist.A perfect evening snack or starter.

Ingredients:
  • 1 Bread Loaf or French bread or Regular bread
  • 1 Onion-finely chopped
  • 1 Tomato-finely chopped
  • 2 Green chillies-finely chopped
  • Coriander leaves-finely chopped
  • 2 Lemons
  • 1 tsp Olive oil
  • 1 tsp Chaat Masala
  • 3-4 Papad
  • 250 gms Butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper powder-optional
Method:
  • Cut the bread loaf into slices and toast it in butter in a grill pan.
  • Roast the papad over stove and keep aside.
  • Add the finely chopped onions,tomatoes, green chillies,coriander leaves,chaat masala,lemon juice, olive oil and salt.Stir well.
  • Spoon the mixture on top of toast and also add some crushed roasted papad over it and serve.

Note:You can sprinkle some grated mozzarella cheese over this before baking.
You may also add small chunks of paneer.

GUACAMOLE DIP-FAMOUS MEXICAN DIP

Nov 7th, 2016 12:39 pm

Guacamole is a healthy and delicious snack enjoyed with tortilla chips,crackers or any chips. It can be served along with lamb chops as well.Do make this healthy homemade dip.

Ingredients:
  • 2 Ripe avocados
  • 2 Onions medium-finely chopped
  • 1 cup Tomatoes-chopped
  • 1 tsp Red chilli sauce
  • Coriander leaves- chopped
  • Jalapeno -seeded and chopped(optional)
  • 1/2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • Garlic 4-5 cloves-crushed
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder or to taste
  • Salt to taste
Method:
  • Cut the avocado into half and discard the seed. Scoop out flesh using a spoon.
  • Using a fork mash well.Blend together avocado, onions, garlic cloves, lemon juice, salt, black peppercorns and jalapeno to a fine paste.
  • Serve with tortilla chips or serve as a side dish with lamb chops.

PHIRNI

Oct 19th, 2016 4:51 pm

Light, creamy and sweet, this chilled rice pudding flavored with rose water is the perfect accompaniment for a hot summer day or after a spicy meal. Top it with chopped nuts for added indulgence. You can even add saffron (kesar) too.

Ingredients:
  • 3 cups (450 ml) Milk
  • 1 can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 100 gm Rice
  • 1 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 1 tsp Rose Water
Method:
  • Soak rice in water for 30 minutes to 1 hour. Drain water and grind to a fine paste.
  • Add milk to paste and heat together, stirring constantly so that the mixture does not stick to the bottom. Bring to a boil. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
  • Add sweetened condensed milk and remove from stove.
  • Add cardamon powder and rose water. Leave to cool and set in a refrigerator. Serve garnished with chopped nuts.

Enjoy the refreshing dessert!

MUTTON BONE SOUP

Apr 15th, 2016 11:44 pm

Mutton bones soup is a flavourful, tangy and spicy soup made of tender mutton bones and fat cooked with aromatic spices.This dish goes well with cooked naan or rice. This soup is so delicious and is a must-have if you are suffering from severe cold and throat infection.It soothes you immediately!

Ingredients:
  • 1 pound Mutton with tender bones
  • 1 Tomato, chopped finely -optional
  • 1 Onion -finely chopped or Shallots(6-8)
  • Chicken stock powder/Vegetable stock powder - 1 cube/2 tbsp -(optional)
  • 5 Garlic pods
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Whole black pepper
  • 3-4 Dry Red chillies
  • 1 tsp Coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • Bunch of Curry leaves
  • Coriander and Mint leaves - chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups Water
Method:
  • Wash the mutton pieces thoroughly.
  • In a pan, dry roast dry red chillies, whole black pepper, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, garlic and shallot. Grind all these ingredients to make a coarse paste.
  • Heat a deep bottomed pan or pressure cooker and add mutton, onion, tomato, turmeric powder,chicken stock powder,curry leaves, salt and 4 glass of water and bring it to boil.
  • Once the mutton pieces get cooked, add the coarse mixture to the soup and and mix well.Once cooked switch off the stove .
  • Add coriander and mint leaves and lemon juice. Serve hot with any type of naan.

BASBOUSA(SEMOLINA CAKE)

Apr 15th, 2016 4:36 pm

Semolina Cake, popularly known as Basbousa are small cakes made of semolina soaked in sugar syrup. These are perfect with coffee or tea.

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/4 cup Semolina
  • 1 1/2 cup Dessicated coconut
  • 1/2 cup All purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • Butter (melted)
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup Milk or 1 cup Yogurt
  • Saffron ½ g
  • 1 tsp Cardamon-optional
  • 3 drops Almond essence
  • Sugar Syrup:
    • 1 cup Sugar
    • 1 cup Water
    • 1/2 tsp Lemon juice
    • 1 tsp Rose water
Method:
  • Take a bowl mix butter, semolina, desiccated coconut, all purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, butter, egg, milk with saffron and almond essence into a batter.
    • If the batter is very thick add a little of milk.
  • Butter or oil your pan or oven proof casserole and add the batter in it then level it off and cut it any shape you like.
  • Decorate with almonds.
  • Bake at 350 degreesF for 30 to 40 minutes depends on your oven temperature till golden color on top.
  • Pour the warm sugar syrup on the top of the basbousa.
  • Preparing Sugar Syrup:
    • In a saucepan add sugar,water and lemon juice.
    • Cook over medium to low heat until the mixture comes to boil.
    • Continue boiling for 5-10 minutes till the syrup is thick.
    • Add rose water and then let it cool down.

AVOCADO MILKSHAKE WITHOUT ICE CREAM

Apr 6th, 2016 4:30 pm

A healthy and creamy shake made from only 3 ingredients:avocado,milk and sugar. Avocado,also known as Butter Fruit, is rich in vitamin, potassium, magnesium and fiber. Avocado flesh has creamy texture and hence, its milkshake doesn’t need ice cream to bring creaminess. Enjoy it as an afternoon summer drink or healthy breakfast drink.

Ingredients:
  • 1 large Ripe Avocado
  • 2 cups Milk or Almond Milk
  • 3 tbsp Sugar or to taste
Method:
  • Choose ripe avocado by pressing it gently; if it is hard, it means Avocado is unripe; if it’s too soft then it means Avocado is overripe; if it is soft yet little firm, then it means Avocado is ready. Cut it into half and remove the pit
  • Scoop the pulp out using a spoon and place it into a blender jar. Add milk and sugar.
  • Blend until smooth texture. Taste it for sugar at this stage and add more sugar if desired. Grind again for few seconds.
  • Pour it into serving glasses and serve immediately for immediate consumption.
  • Sweet and creamy Avocado Milkshake is now ready to serve.

Tips and Variations:
* Use almond milk or soy milk or thin coconut milk instead of dairy milk to make the Vegan Avocado Shake .
* Consume the milkshake immediately. As time passes, it would start to become dark due to oxidization.
* Add few ice-cubes while blending in step-3 for an extra chilled drink.

CHAPLI KABAB

Mar 16th, 2016 7:23 pm

These super moist Chapli Kababs are seriously yummy and juicy made with mince meat and spices.

Ingredients:
  • 1 kg Mince meat
  • 2 Onions- finely chopped
  • 2-3 Tomatoes- finely chopped
  • 5-6 Green chillies -finely chopped as per taste
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Paprika
  • 1 tbsp Coriander seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 3 tbsp Gram Flour (besan)
  • 1 tbsp Pomegrante powder (anardana)
  • 1 tbsp Garam masala
  • Coriander leaves-finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
  • 2-3 tbsp Cornmeal, Rice flour or Breadcrumbs-optional
Method:
  • In a bowl add all the ingredients together and mix with your hands really well until everything is fully combined.
  • Place cling film wrap over the bowl and place in the fridge for 1/2 hour to chill. Do not leave it in the fridge too long as the tomatoes start to release their water and will make the kebabs soggy.
  • Heat some oil in a small non-stick frying pan.
  • Wet your hands with water and take a handful amount of mixture and form into flat round large patties.
  • Place the kebabs on to the frying pan and shallow fry on a high heat for approx 3-4 mins. Do not move the kebab, let it cook and form a crust and continue cooking the kababs until the color is nice and dark.
  • After about 3 mins turn the kebab over and cook again for 3-4 minutes again letting it form a crust on the other side.
  • Remove from the pan and drain on kitchen towel and drizzle some lemon juice on them before serving.
  • Serve with some mint chutney or ketchup.onions, tomatoes and/or a saladEat with naan or as a side dish.
  • Serve in a burger with a cheese slice,tomato slices,onion and salad.

Note:You can even shape the patties and place them on a tray, and place it in the freezer for half an hour so they firm up. Then proceed to fry them directly from the freezer,this helps maintain their shape. :)

PASTA WITH CORIANDER(CILANTRO) PESTO

Mar 7th, 2016 3:38 pm

I enjoy making pasta, in any form and this recipe using coriander(cilantro) pesto is very easy to make.Enjoy this delicious pasta.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Penne pasta
  • 1 cup Coriander leaves chopped
  • 2 tbsp Almond powder
  • 1 tsp Ground Green chillies
  • 1 tsp Garlic
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 125ml Fresh cream
  • Parmesan cheese-grated
  • Peppers-diced
  • Salt to taste
Method:
  • Boil penne pasta with salt and little oil till done.
  • For pesto-blend together coriander leaves, almond powder, ground green chillies, garlic,lemon juice and water.
  • Saute 1 tsp crushed garlic in butter. Add in a few diced peppers and saute till soft.
  • Add in pesto and fresh cream.Add salt and bring to boil.
  • Add in pasta.Sprinkle grated parmesan on top and serve hot along with garlic bread.

LAMB BRAIN FRY(BHEJA FRY)RECIPE

Mar 2nd, 2016 9:42 pm

Bheja fry or lamb brain fry masala is one such popular and mouth-watering dish. It’s such a tasty recipe which is quick in making and good side dish for roti, naan, bread.

Ingredients:
  • 1 Lamb brain/sheep brain
  • 1 cup Onion- finely sliced
  • 1 tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Coriander powder-optional
  • 1 tsp Lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp Oil
Method:
  • Wash the brain thoroughly,soak the brain in hot water for 10 minutes this helps in cleaning. Remove the brains from the water and carefully remove membrane or nerves.Chop the brains into medium pieces.
  • Marinate the brain with salt, ginger and garlic paste ,red chili powder, turmeric powder and garam masala powder.
  • Heat oil in a frying pan and add sliced onions.Saute the onions till they turn golden brown.Remove half the onions and keep them aside.
  • Next add the brain.Stir well and cover with lid and cook on low flame for 8-10 minutes.Don’t mix it often,you may lose the good look and texture.
  • When the oil separates , add chopped coriander and mint leaves ,squeeze lemon juice and also add remaining brown onion.
  • Serve hot with roti or naan.

KNAFEH CUPCAKE-ARABIC SWEET CHEESE PASTRY

Feb 27th, 2016 5:43 pm

Knafeh is a Middle Eastern dessert,where cheese pastry is soaked in sweet sugar-based syrup and topped with pistachios. An easy recipe to prepare cupcake knafeh dessert.

Ingredients:
  • For the Crust :
    • 1/2 package Shredded Phyllo Dough (Kataif)
    • 1 stick unsalted Butter melted (1/2 cup)
    • 1/4 tsp Orange Color (or Ziyad brand Kunafa)
  • For the Cream Filling (keshta):
    • 2 cups Whole Milk
    • 1 tbsp Cornstarch
    • 1 tbsp Flour
    • 1 tbsp Sugar
    • 1/2 can Cream (Nestle crema) (225 ml) or 1/2 cup creamy Ricotta Cheese.
    • 1 cup Shredded Mozzarella
  • For the Syrup:
    • 2 cups Sugar
    • 1 cup Water
    • 2 tsp Rose Water
    • 1 tsp Lemon juice or 1/8 tsp Citric acid (Lemondoze)
Method:
  • Preparing Crust:
    • Chop Fillo dough very small with a sharp knife or you can use food processor to do so.
    • Mix melted butter and the Kunafa color together and then pour it over the chopped phyllo dough.
    • Mix very well till all the shredded phyllo dough is covered with butter and colored.
  • Preparing Cream Filling:
    • Mix milk, cornstarch, flour and sugar and whisk very well until the starch and the flour dissolve very well.
    • Cook over low heat till the mixture is thickened.
    • Add the cream and mix well then leave aside to cool down.
    • Cover and refrigerator after it cools down.
  • Preparing Sugar Syrup:
    • In a saucepan add sugar,water and lemon juice.
    • Cook over medium to low heat until the mixture comes to boil.
    • Continue boiling for 5-10 minutes till the syrup is thick.
    • Add rose water and then let it cool down.
  • Prepare cupcake moulds by greasing with butter or place cupcake papers in moulds.
  • Put a layer of the shredded Kunafa in the cupcake moulds and press tightly.
  • Spread a little amount of Mozzarella cheese then top it with little of cream (keshta) then spread more Mozzarella cheese.
  • Finally distribute the remaining of the shredded Fillo dough in each mould and press carefully.
  • Bake in 350 F preheated oven, on middle rack of the oven, for about 30 minutes or till the bottom starts to be golden.
  • Immediately pour the cold syrup on the hot kunafa and cover so the kunafa becomes soft.
  • Decorate with chopped pistachios and serve warm.

Note: 1-Instead of the cream filling you can use akawi cheese( sweet Arabic cheese). 2-Instead of the cream you can use creamy Ricotta cheese + Mozzarella cheese.
