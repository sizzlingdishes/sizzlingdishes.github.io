TOMATO MUTTON

Oct 2nd, 2012 12:00 am

A mild mutton curry, with a fragrant tomato gravy.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 kg Mutton
  • 1 Onion -finely chopped
  • 2 Tomatoes-finely chopped
  • 1tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1tbsp Red chilli powder
  • 1/4tbsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 Green chillies
  • 1tbsp lemon juice-optional
  • Salt to taste
  • 4tbsp oil
  • Coriander leaves-finely chopped
  • Mint leaves-finely chopped
Method:
  • Wash and marinate the mutton with ginger-garlic paste,salt,red chilli powder,turmeric powder and keep aside.
  • Take a heavy bottomed pan,add oil at medium heat and add onion;fry till they are slightly golden in color.
  • Add the marinated mutton and mix well and let it cook for few minutes.
  • Now add the chopped tomatoes and green chillies and let it cook till it gets smooth and mushy.
  • Now add water and cook until the mutton is tender.
  • Then leave it on for low heat till the thin layer of oil appears on the bottom surface and cover the pan and leave till the gravy is thick.
  • Add coriander and mint leaves.
  • Remove from heat and serve with parathas and plain rice.

CHICKEN BIRYANI-II

Oct 1st, 2012 12:00 am

This is a good recipe for a Chicken Biryani,a popular spiced Indian rice.

My hubby loved it and complimented me by saying it tasted like restaurant biryani..:).So do try out.

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2kg Chicken
  • 4 cups Basmati Rice
  • 3 large Onions sliced
  • 2 tbsp Ghee/1 cup Oil
  • 1 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  • 1 tbsp Green chilli paste
  • 1 tsp Ajinomoto
  • 1 tbsp Biryani Masala-grind cinnamon,cloves,cardamon and shahzeera
  • Shaan Biryani Packet-for extra taste
  • Coriander-finely chopped
  • Mint leaves-finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Saffron dissolved in hot water
  • For Rice
    • 1 tbsp Shahzeera/Caraway seeds
    • 4 Black Peppercorns
    • 4 Green Cardamons
    • 2 Cinnamon Sticks
    • 2 Cloves
    • Salt to taste
Method:
    • In a large sauce pan at medium heat ,add 2tbsp ghee,add onions and fry till golden brown and keep aside.
    • In a large bowl,add the chicken and marinate with ginger-garlic paste,red chilli powder,turmeric powder,garam masala powder,ajinomoto,green chilli paste,,biryani masala,yogurt,fried onions,finely chopped coriander and mint leaves,salt and 3tbsp oil.
    • Mix the chicken well and cover and leave it for 3hours or more.
    • Next wash the rice and soak it in surlpus water for about 1/2 hour.
    • Meanwhile,in a large cooking pot or rice cooker;pour in water along with cloves,cardamon,cinnamon sticks,shazeera and salt.
    • Let it come to boil,add the drained rice.Give it a stir and let the rice cook until it is half done,2-3 minutes.Drain the rice.
    • Now take a large vessel/non stick vessel;add the marinated chicken.Spread the drained cooked rice over the chicken.
    • Spread evenly the fried onions and saffron color.
    • Then cover the vessel lid with newspaper or aluminium foil;and place a heavy weighted vessel over it so that no steam can escape.
    • Place the biryani vessel on stove and cook on low flame.
    • Allow to cook for 10-15 mins.
    • Serve the delicious biryani with dahi chutney and mirch salan.

COLD COFFEE

Sep 29th, 2012 12:00 am

Well I am not much in love with hot coffee but I enjoy cold coffee more.So here goes the cooling and refreshing coffee drink.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups Vanilla Ice cream
  • 1/2 cup Chilled Milk
  • Sugar to taste
  • 1tbsp Coffee
  • 1tbsp Cream-optional
  • Ice cubes 2
Method:
    • In a blender, blend 2cups of vanilla ice cream,chilled milk,sugar,coffee and ice cubes until frothy and creamy.
    • Now take a glass pour the cold coffee mixture and then you can add 1 scoop of ice cream or cream on top.
    • Cold Coffee is ready to enjoy.

Enjoy :)

Note:If you don’t like vanilla ice-cream,you can make the coffee by skipping the ice-cream or can use any other flavored ice-cream.

KABSA -I

Sep 27th, 2012 12:00 am

Kabsa is a tradional dish of Saudi Arabia.There are different ways to prepare but this is easiest way to prepare.It’s my all time favorite and this taste great when served with chicken.

Ingredients:
  • 2cups Basmati Rice
  • 3cups water
  • 4 Green cardamons
  • 4 Cloves
  • 4 Black pepper corns
  • 1 Chicken cube stock
  • 2 Carrots grated
  • 10 Raisins
  • Oil
Method:
  • Put oil in a pan and add cloves,cardamons,black pepper corns and fry for 2-3 minutes on medium heat.
  • Add chicken cube and cook for 2 minutes.
  • Now add rice and water and cook the rice with lid on.
  • In a separate pan,add oil and fry the grated carrots for 3minutes and keep aside.And in the same oil fry the raisins for 2 minutes.
  • After the rice has been cooked,add the fried carrots and raisins.
  • Serve rice with tomato sauce.

VEG SCHEZWAN NOODLES

Sep 24th, 2012 12:00 am

Veg Schezwan Noodles are spicy,tasty and delicious Chinese dish,ready in few minutes.The easy recipe of schezwan veg noodles is here.

Ingredients:
  • 1 packet Spagethi
  • 2tbsp home made Schezwan sauce/Ching’s Secret Schezwan sauce
  • 1tbsp Soya sauce
  • 1 Carrot diced
  • 1 Green bell pepper sliced
  • 1 Red bell pepper sliced
  • 1 Onion sliced
  • 1 Spring onion chopped
  • 4-5 French beans diced-optional
  • 1/4 medium Cabbage ,shredded
  • 1tbsp Vinegar
  • Minced ginger and garlic
  • 2tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
Method:
  • Boil the water with little salt and then add the spagethi and boil them.
  • Once the noodles are done,drain the water and add sesame oil to keep the noodles soft and separate.
  • Cut all the veggies into fine julience.
  • Heat oil in frying pan.Add the minced ginger and garlic.
  • Now add all the vegetables and cook on high heat.
  • Add vinger,soya sauce and salt and stir well.
  • Add noodles and mix well
  • Serve hot and garnish with spring onions.

MINI VEG PIZZA-I

Sep 20th, 2012 12:00 am

Who doesn’t love pizza with less chesse and your favorite toppings. This recipe is very easy and quick to make in a different style without making pizza dough in less time. So check out

Ingredients:
  • 2 Pizza Breads/Indian Naan
  • 2 cups Tomato sauce
  • Few Crushed Garlic
  • 1tbsp Black pepper
  • Toppings:
    • 1 Onion chopped
    • 1 Green pepper/capsicum, sliced thin or diced
    • 1 Red pepper, sliced thin or diced
    • 1 Tomatoes, sliced
    • 1/2cup Black olives
    • 1/2cup Pineapple chunks-optional
    • 1/2cup Jalapenos
    • 2 cups grated Mozzarella cheese
    • 1 cup Pizza cheddar cheese-grated
Method:
  • Place a pizza bread on the greased pizza tray. Preheat the oven to 450°F for at least 30 minutes.
  • Prepare the pizza sauce by mixing the tomato sauce,crushed garlic and black pepper.
  • Spread the pizza sauce as evenly as you can on the pizza and sprinkle the mozzarella cheese.
  • Now place your desired toppings on the pizza;don’t overload the pizza with lots of toppings.
  • Again sprinkle the mozzarella and cheddar cheese on top.
  • Place the pizza in the oven and bake it in a pre-heated oven at 350’F for about 20-25mins.
  • Let it cool for few minutes after it is done.
  • If you want spicy pizza,sprinkle chilli flakes on top.
  • Enjoy the home made mini pizza with your family.

CRISPY FRIED CHICKEN

Sep 18th, 2012 12:00 am

Crispy fried chicken is a dry side dish that can be served with parathas or white rice.Its very easy to prepare and tastes good.This is regular item at my home.

Ingredients:
  • Boneless chicken/Chicken legs
  • 1 tsp. ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 2 tsp Coriander powder -optional
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil-deep fry
  • Cilantro leaves (finely chopped)-garnish
  • 1 Onion (sliced long)-garnish
Method:
  • Clean the chicken and marinate the chicken with ginger-garlic paste,salt,turmeric powder,red chilli powder and coriander powder and keep aside for few hours.
  • Heat the pan and add oil and then deep fry the chicken by turning around.Serve hot
  • Enjoy the crispy fried chicken.:)

BUTTER PANEER

Sep 18th, 2012 12:00 am

Butter Mutter Paneer, also known as Paneer Makhni is the vegetarian version of the popular Butter Chicken. This dish can be served at parties and family gatherings.This goes well with naans,parathas and white rice. If you are health conscious person so reduce the intake.

Ingredients:
  • Paneer
  • 4tbsp Butter
  • 2tbsp Oil
  • 1tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1cup crushed tomatoes
  • 1tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1tsp Coriander powder
  • 1tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Whipping cream
  • 1cup Water
  • 1tsp Sugar-optional
  • Cilantro-to garnish
Method:
  • Cut paneer to desired shape.
  • Heat a pan and add oil and then butter.Once hot add ginger-garlic paste and saute for 2 minutes.
  • Add in the crushed tomatoes.Add the spices;coriander powder,garam masala,red chilli powder and salt.
  • Mix and cook till oil separates.
  • Add paneer and mix gently.Pour 1cup water and simmer for 5minutes.
  • Lower heat and add cream.Allow it to come to boil.
  • Add sugar and mix well.
  • Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

VANILLA SPONGE CAKE

Sep 15th, 2012 12:00 am

I baked this cake for my lovely sis’s back there in India.It really turned out too good and spongy..

Ingredients:
  • Maida 2 cups
  • Eggs 2
  • Sugar 60gms
  • Vanilla Essence 1tsp
Method:
    • Firstly separate egg yolk and egg white into two separate bowls.
    • Now beat egg white with beater or whisker.
    • Take about 60 gms of sugar and add it to egg white and beat it until sugar dissolves.
    • Preheat oven to 180’c
    • Next add the egg yolk and beat it and also add 1tsp vanilla essece .
    • Now add 2 cups maida and beat well so to form the cake batter.
    • Take spatulla and mix it lightly.
    • Finally take a cake mould,spread the butter on all sides and place the batter and tap it to level.
    • Bake for about 20mins and Set for 5 mins.
    • Then cut into slices and serve with ice-cream,fruits or cherries.

Enjoy :)

 

FRIED MEAT

Sep 13th, 2012 12:00 am

This is a slightly spicy dry side-dish.Extremely simple,it tastes great when paired with pulao.

Ingredients:
  • Meat
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 2-3 Green chillies
  • 1tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala powder
  • Curry leaves
  • Lemon juice
Method:
  • Clean and cut the meat into even sized cubes.
  • Marinate meat with ginger-garlic paste,salt,turmeric powder,red chilli powder,coriander powder and garam masala.Keep aside atleast for half an hour.
  • Saute the onions till golden brown and then add curry leaves and green chillies.
  • Next add the marinated meat and half a cup of water.Cover and cook on low heat till the meat is tender.
  • Once the meat is well cooked, stir-fry continuously till it turns dry and dark brown in colour.
  • Once done add lemon juice and serve with onions or capsicum.
