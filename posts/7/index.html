ACHARI MURGH

Feb 14th, 2013 12:00 am

Achari Murgh is a spicy and hot Chicken that is cooked in spicy North Indian style.

Ingredients:
  • Chicken-boneless or with bones
  • Onions- 1 chopped
  • Ginger and Garlic paste- 2tbsp
  • Cumin powder- 1tsp
  • Coriander powder- 2tsp
  • Red chilli powder- 3tsp
  • Salt
  • Curd -1tbsp
  • Fenugreek seeds- 1/4th tsp
  • Mustard seeds-1 tsp
  • Curry leaves
  • Dry mango powder(amchur)- 1 tsp
  • Red chilli flakes - 4-5
  • Green chillies- 4-5 (slit)
Method:
  • Take chicken and marinate it with ginger garlic paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, salt and curd.
  • Take fenugreek seeds, mustard seeds,some curry leaves(4-5), dry mango powder and chilli flakes in a grinder. Make a dry paste without water.Add to chicken and leave for one hour.
  • Take some oil( 3-4 tbsp) in a pan. Add some dried red chillies, mustard seeds, curry leaves and stir well. When done add onions and saute for 4-5 mins. Add ginger garlic paste (1tsp), coriander cumin powder-(1tsp) and saute’. Drop in 4-5 slit green chillies too.
  • Add chicken and fry for 4-5 min.
  • Now add little water enough to tenderize chicken and cover the lid.
  • Once done, garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

CHICKEN SPRING ROLLS

Feb 12th, 2013 12:00 am

Spring rolls are light easy appetizer with an exotic flavor.These are easy to make so do check out.

Spring Roll Wrapper Packet

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 pound Ground Chicken
  • 1/2 head of Small Cabbage, shredded
  • 2 Carrots, thin julienne cut
  • 1 Stalk Green Onion, chopped or Onion chopped
  • 1/2 Green,Red,Yellow Bell Peppers,thin julienne cut
  • 2 tsp Soya Sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Sesame Seeds
  • 1 tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Chilli sauce or Red chilli flakes
  • 1/4 tsp Ajinomoto-optional
  • 1 tsp Vinegar
  • Cornstarch or Cornflour-optional
  • 1 tsp All Purpose Flour or 1/2 egg white wash
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • Salt to taste
  • 50 Spring roll wrappers, defrosted
  • Oil, for deep frying
Method:
    • In a large bowl, combine the soy sauce,pepper and cornstarch. Add in the chicken and mix well. Let marinate for 10 minutes (or up to overnight in the refrigerator).
    • Heat a wok or large saute pan over high heat. When hot, add 1 tbsp of the cooking oil.Stir fry the ground chicken until browned. Remove browned ground chicken from wok to a bowl and set aside.
    • Wipe the wok clean and turn heat to medium. When just starting to get hot, add the remaining cooking oil. Add in the green onion,ginger and garlic paste and cook for 30 seconds.
    • Add in the carrots,green bell pepper,red bell pepper,yellow bell pepper and cabbage. Stir well and turn the heat to medium-high. Stir fry the vegetables for 2 minutes,or until the veggies have softened.
    • Add the cooked ground chicken back into the wok, stir well. Add in the soya sauce,chilli sauce or red chilli flakes,sesame seeds,vinegar,ajinomotto and toss again.Cook until the liquid dries off.
    • In a small bowl,add water and all purpose flour and make a smooth paste. Open the spring roll sheet package, cover with barely damp towel to prevent drying out.
    • Add 1 tablespoon of filling to spring roll and roll up. Secure the edge with all purpose flour paste. Keep rolled spring rolls covered with plastic wrap or in ziploc bag to prevent drying.
    •  To fry, heat oil in a wok or deep.Carefully slide in the spring rolls, one at a time, to the oil to fry. Turn the spring rolls to brown evenly and fry for about 3 minutes. Let cool on paper towel.
    • Serve hot with ketchup or Chinese hot sauce.

Enjoy :)

EGG BIRYANI

Feb 8th, 2013 12:00 am

Egg Biryani, absolutely delicious recipe! A simple to make super recipe of Egg Biryani, that your family will love. An easy and great dish for egg lovers.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 tsp Biryani Masala powder
  • Ghee
  • 6 eggs
  • Water
  • 1 tbsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 4 Green Chillies
  • Oil
  • 1 Onion large
  • 2 cup Plain Yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices-cardamon,cloves,cinnamon stick,bay leaves and shazeera
  • Fresh Coriander/Cilantro Leaves and Mint leaves-finely chopped
Method:
  • Wash and soak rice for atleast ½ hour.
  • Next boil the eggs and then shell the boiled eggs and give four long cuts on the white portion of each egg without separating it.
  • In a khadhai or pot, heat the ghee or oil.Add whole garam masala spices and onions,fry the onions until they become golden brown and then add ginger and garlic paste ,green chillies ,mint leaves,coriander leaves,biryani masala and curd and cook.
  • And then add 1 1/2 times the amount of water to rice and bring it to boil.
  • Next add the soaked rice and mix well and cook covered till rice is done.
  • Meanwhile take a small bowl add red chilli powder,salt,gram flour(besan), 1 beaten egg and mix well.Next Dip each boiled egg into the mixture and keep aside.
  • Take a pan add butter and fry the eggs for few mins and then remove and keep aside.
  • Now check the rice,when the air holes appear on the rice surface, reduce the stove heat to low. Cook until all of the water has absorbed and the rice is tender.
  • When the rice is cooked top it with eggs.
  • Serve hot.

PANCAKE

Feb 4th, 2013 12:00 am

Tall, fluffy pancakes are delicious served with maple syrup or honey or top with strawberries and whipped cream for a real treat.

Ingredients:
  • 1 Cup All purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  • Dash of Salt
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 tbsp Melted Butter
  • Few drops of Vanilla essence
  • Maple syrup or Honey
Method:
  • In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  • Using a whisk or large fork, mix in the milk and egg until well combined and smooth. Stir in the melted butter.
  • Heat non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Using a 1/4 cup measure, pour pancake batter onto hot griddle. When pancake is golden brown, flip to cook other side.
  • Serve warm with honey or maple syrup,whipped cream and any fruit compote of your choice.

VEGGIE PULAO

Jan 16th, 2013 12:00 am

Vegetable pulao makes a delicious and simple main course rice preparation. Best served with raita.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 3 cups Warm Water
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Ghee-clarified Butter
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 2 Cinnamon sticks
  • Bay leaves
  • Cloves
  • 1/2 cup Onion-finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Green and Red bell pepper-finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Green chilli paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cups Diced Carrots
  • 1/2 cup Green peas
  • 1/2 cup Beans
  • Saffron strands
Method:
  • Wash,rinse and soak the Basmati rice in water for 20 mins.
  • Take a pan and add ghee-clarified butter or oil,add cumin seeds,cinnanom stick ,cloves,bay leaves and then chopped onions.Cook the onions till transclucent.
  • Next add green and red bell peppers and let it cook and then add ginger and garlic paste and green chilli paste.
  • Now add the veggies-carrots,green peas and beans.Cover the pan and cook for few mins till the veggies are half done.
  • Next add rice and salt and give a quick stir.Let it cook for few mins.
  • Then add warm water and few saffron strands.Cover and cook till the rice are completely done on low heat.
  • Serve hot with raita or pickle.

FRUITS CUSTARD

Jan 11th, 2013 12:00 am

Fruits Custard is a very delicious and healthy dessert with the creamy texture of the custard and crunch of the fruits in between.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 litre Milk
  • 2 tbsp Custard powder
  • Sugar
  • Raw fruits Apple/Banana/Grapes/Oranges/Pomegranate/Mango-Finely chopped
  • Dry Fruits (optional)
Method:
  • Take a saucepan,add milk and bring to boil and then add sugar and stir till it dissolves.
  • Next in a small bowl add custard powder in a small amount of of milk,mix it well without any lumps.
  • Add this custard paste into the boilng milk and keep stirring till its creamy and thicken on a low flame.
  • Remove from heat when it reaches the desired consistency.
  • Allow the custard to cool thoroughly.
  • Finally in a serving dish add the chopped fruits and then pour over the custard cream and refrigerate.
  • Serve cool and Enjoy it!!

EGG PUFF

Jan 7th, 2013 12:00 am

Puffs are the easiest snack to make and it reminds us of the college days where we use to grab these during break time.Do try out and enjoy!!

Ingredients:
    • Boiled Eggs 4
    • Black Pepper
    • Puff Pastry Sheets
    • Salt
    • Chaat Masala
    • Garam Masala powder-optional
    • Egg wash
Method:
  • Take boiled eggs and cut into half and then sprinkle pepper powder,chat masala,garam masala ,if needed salt.
  • Take puff pastry sheet,place the sprinkled egg on top and close the sheet by applying little water on the edges.
  • Brush the puffs with egg wash for better color.
  • Bake it in the oven for 30 mins at 350 degrees.
  • Once it is done serve with sauce or ketchup.

TOMATO CHUTNEY

Jan 3rd, 2013 12:00 am

Spicy and tangy chutney.Simple to prepare no need of grinder.Tastes good with anything.

Ingredients:
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 Green chillies
  • 4 Curry leaves
  • 1/2 tbsp Cumin seeds (Zeera)
  • 1/2 tbsp Black Mustard seeds(Rai)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil
Method:
  • In a large non-stick saucepan/Khadhai add oil at medium heat.Add cumin seeds, black mustard seeds & curry leaves fry for 30 seconds.
  • Then add tomatoes, ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, turmeric powder,green chilli and mix well.
  • Now after a couple of minutes add salt,do not cook on a high heat,simmer heat and cover the pan leave till smooth.
  • At this stage there will be some liquid from the Tomato in the pan. You need to now stir it till tomatoes gets smooth and mushy.
  • Then cover the pan let it until all the liquid evaporates,leave till done.Remove from the heat.
  • Serve this as side dish with rice and parathas.

MUTTON BIRYANI

Jan 2nd, 2013 12:00 am

Flavorful rice cooked in very tasty, delicious mutton stock. Its aroma is unbeatable when this biryani is ready!

Ingredients:
  • 1lb Mutton cut into big pieces
  • 2cusp Basmati Rice
  • Mint Leaves and Coriander leaves -finely chopped
  • 2tbsp Oil
  • 2tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1/2tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1tsp Lemon juice
  • Garam Masala powder
  • 1tsp Shazeera
  • Whole Garam Masala-Cloves,Cardamons,Cinnamon sticks ,Bay leaves
  • 1 big Onion-diced thinly
  • Salt to taste
  • 6 Green Chillies
  • Saffron strands
  • Milk
Method:
  • Wash and soak rice in enough water for 30 mins.
  • Wash mutton well and cut in to big pieces. Add ginger and garlic paste in to a bowl, turmeric powder, lemon juice, mint leaves, garam masala powder, coriander leaves, mix well, add mutton pieces in to it, mix well, cover and marinate for 3 hours.
  • And then add marinated mutton into pressure cooker, then add green chillies, salt to taste, 2 cups water and cook till meat is tender.
  • In another pan,heat oil and add onion and fry it till golden color.And then take them out and let it cool for 5 mins and then crush them finely.
  • Add half of onions to the mutton and mix it.
  • In a cooking pot,add water,cloves,cardamons,shazeera,cinnamon sticks and bay leaves and some coriander and mint leaves and bring the water to boil.
  • Once the water starts boiling add the soaked rice and boil it.Once the rice is cooked,drain the excess water and take out rice in a different dish.
  • Now in the main cooking pot,layer half of the rice at bottom and then layer of mutton and then top it with a layer of rice again.
  • Take saffron stands mixed in milk and sprinkle it on the top of rice.Add crushed and fried onions.Cover the lid and leave it on simmer for 10-15 mins.
  • Serve with raita, salad or pickle!
