TANDOORI CHICKEN

Aug 30th, 2013 12:00 am

Chicken Tandoori is a popular dish marinated in yogurt, lemon juice and aromatic Indian spices and then grilled in a Tandoor or in an oven.An extremely popular mughlai delicacy which can be served at dinner party.

This is the first time I’ve made tandoori chicken after seeing a couple of gorgeous tandoori chicken recipes. I used chicken drumsticks and thighs for the preparation as I didn’t get a whole chicken.Do try out and enjoy the juicy and moist chicken tandoori.

Ingredients:
  • For the Inner Coating:
    • 1 Whole Chicken or Chicken wings or Drumsticks
    • 1 tbsp Red chili powder
    • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
    • 1 tbsp Oil
    • 1 tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • For the Tandoori Masala Marination:
    • 1/2 cup Yogurt
    • 1 tbsp Red chili Powder or as per spicy level
    • 1 tbsp Kasoori Methi
    • 1 tsp Garam Masala
    • 1 tsp  Cumin powder
    • 1 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 tbsp readymade Tandoori Masala
    • 1 tbsp Corn Flour for dusting-optional
Method:
  • Wash off whole chicken inside out or drumsticks and then pat dry using paper towel. Slit the pieces for perfect marination.
  • Mix red chili powder, lemon juice, salt, ginger garlic paste,oil in a bowl and mix well and apply the masala to the inside of chicken where slits made as well as inside cavity. Keep it to rest for 15 minutes.
  • Next take thick yogurt, to that add red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, kasoori methi,tandoori masala, salt, oil, mix well. Add red food color if desired. Now apply this tandoori masala paste well on all over chicken. Cover and keep it in refrigerator overnight.
  • Remove chicken 30 minutes before you want to bake it. Dust corn flour using tea strainer all over chicken. Brush some oil on the top of it.
  • Bake at 400 degree Fahrenheit for 25 minutes After 25 minutes flip the chicken and again bake for 20 more minutes. After 20 minutes again flip chicken one more time and bake for final 20 minutes.
  • Serve hot with salad and garnish with red onion and lemon.

CHICKEN TIKKA MASALA

Aug 30th, 2013 12:00 am

Chicken Tikka Masala is a flavorful silky sauce with cream,tomatoes and grilled chicken. Not too hot, but rich and creamy.

Ingredients:
  • For the Marination:
    • 1 lb Chicken, cut into cubes or Boneless chicken
    • 1 cup Yogurt
    • 1 tbsp Cumin powder
    • 1/2 tbsp Coriander powder
    • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
    • 2 tbsp readymade Tandoori Masala
    • 1 tbsp Garam Masala powder
    • 1 tbsp Ginger and Garlic paste
    • 1 tsp Salt
    • 1/2 tsp Red Chili powder
  • For the Sauce:
    • 1 medium sized Onion, cubed
    • 1/2 roasted Red Bell Pepper (about 1/2 cup when pureed)
    • 6 Cashews
    • 1 tsp Cumin powder
    • 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
    • 3 Cloves, 1/2 inch Cinnamon, 2 Cardamom pods–ground into fine powder
    • 1 tbsp Kasoori Methi (dried Fenugreek leaves, found in Indian grocery stores)
    • 1/4 cup Cream
    • 2 tbsp Tomato ketchup
    • Ready made Chicken Tikka masala packet
    • 1 tbsp Butter
    • 1 tbsp Oil
    • 1/4 tsp of Red Chili powder, or to taste
    • Salt to taste
    • Cilantro for garnish
Method:
  • Marinade the chicken with all the ingredients for marination.Cover and keep it in fridge for about 2 hours.
  • Preheat oven at 200C (400F) and bake the chicken. Keep chicken pieces on oven grill & keep oven tray underneath or put the chicken pieces on soaked skewers. Bake chicken pieces on both sides, each side 8 - 10 min or until golden.
  • If not planning to bake either grill or pan roast the marinated chicken and set aside.
  • In the mean time, roast the onions in the pan or cooker with a few drops of oil until lightly browned.
  • Add the red bell peppers and cashew and toss them around for a couple of minutes. Take if off the stove and cool until ready to handle
  • Blend the mixture into a fine puree. Add a few tablespoons of water if necessary.
  • Heat a pan (you can use the same pan used for roasting) with a tablespoon of oil and a table spoon of butter.
  • When the butter just melts, add all the powders (clove, cinnamon, cardamom powder, cumin and coriander) and saute until their aroma wafts around.
  • Add the red bell pepper, onion and cashew puree and gently saute for 3-4 minutes.
  • Add the chili powder ,salt,tikka masala and the tomato ketchup. Mix well and add the cream.
  • Crush kasoori methi in your hands before adding to the mix and cook for 2 minutes.
  • Now add the grilled chicken pieces and toss to coat well in the sauce.
  • Check taste and adjust salt or chili powder as desired.
  • If you want a thinner consistency add a few more tablespoons of water and a sparing splash of cream. Gently simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Garnish with cilantro and serve with Naans or Basmati rice.

MASOOR DAL(RED LENTIL) CURRY

Aug 21st, 2013 12:00 am

Red Lentils are a good source of potassium,protein and iron.Try this simple and quick way to make home cooked fresh Masoor Dal (Red Lentil) curry.

 

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Masoor Dal (Red Lentils)
  • 1 Onion -finely chopped
  • 1 Tomato-finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Red chili powder
  • 1/4 tbsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 Green chilies
  • Coriander leaves and Mint leaves- finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil
Method:
  • Wash lentils & drain thoroughly.
  • In a large non-stick saucepan or khadhai,add oil at medium heat. Then add onion and fry till they are slightly golden in color.
  • Add ginger and garlic paste, fry for 1 minute.Next add tomato, red chili powder, turmeric powder, green chili and mix well,stir it till tomatoes gets smooth and mushy.
  • Add lentils,1 cup water and add coriander and mint leaves,mix it well.
  • Then cover the pan let it cook until all the liquid evaporates,leave till done.Once done add salt to taste. Remove from the heat.
  • Garnish with coriander and mint leaves.Serve hot with chapatis.

BANANA BERRY SMOOTHIE

Banana Berry smoothie serves something fulfilling drink for hot summers.

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries- finely chopped

SHEER KURMA(VERMICELLI PUDDING)

Aug 12th, 2013 12:00 am

Sheerkurma is a vermicelli pudding that we have always craved for Eid as it is the first thing to have on Eid morning. So enjoy the Eid with festive dessert. Eid Mubarak!!

Ingredients:
  • Vermicelli-broken into pieces
  • 1 litre Whole milk
  • 4 tbsp Evaporated milk
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 4-5 Dates,deseeded and sliced
  • 1/4 cup of nuts assorted and chopped-cashew,raisins,almonds,pistachios
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • pinch of Saffon-optional
  • pinch Cardamon powder or Vanilla essence
Method:
  • Heat the milk and evaporated milk and then add sugar,saffron and cardamon powder.Keep it warm.
  • Heat the ghee until melted and then add the vermicelli and fry on low heat until they turn golden.Remove and set aside.
  • Add the ghee into the pan and fry the raisins,chopped nuts and dates.
  • Once done add the fried vermicelli and nuts into the warm milk.
  • Cook for few minutes until the vermicelli is nice and soft
  • Serve into a bowl and garnish with some nuts and sliced dates.

HAKKA NOODLES

Jul 23rd, 2013 12:00 am

Hakka Noodles is a popular Chinese dish where boiled noodles are stir fried with sauces and vegetables or meats.These noodles are very popular and are loved by all.Hakka noodles is my favorite Chinese food.Enjoy the spicy and colorful noodles.

Ingredients:
  • 1 packet of Hakka Noodles
  • 1 medium Onion-sliced
  • 1/4 small Cabbage-finely shredded
  • Carrot,cut into thin strips or shredded
  • 1 Green Bell pepper,cut into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup Bean sprouts
  • 1 stalk Spring onion ,finely shredded
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp Soya sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp White/Black Pepper powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili sauce-optinal
Method:
  • Boil noodles in sufficient water by adding little salt and oil to it. When the noodles are done,drain and allow them to cool.To prevent noodles from sticking each other add little oil.
  • Heat oil in a non stick wok.Throw in all the vegetables;onion,carrot and green bell pepper and toss.Then add cabbage and stir fry them for couple of minutes.
  • Add salt, pepper powder, soya sauce and toss on high heat.For spiciness add chili sauce.Mix all the sauces well.
  • Next add the boiled noodles to the vegetables.Toss the noodles well.
  • Lastly add the bean sprouts and vinegar and toss again. Serve hot garnished with green spring onions.

GREEN CHICKEN CURRY

Jul 20th, 2013 12:00 am

Green Chicken Curry is a simple and tasty curry.A perfect side dish for chapati and plain rice.

Ingredients:
  • Chicken 1 kg or Boneless chicken
  • Onion 1- chopped
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 tsp Pepper Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Fennel seeds
  • 1/4 cup Yogurt
  • 1/4 cup Heavy Cream (You could also use thick coconut milk instead)
  • 3-4 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Oil
  • Bay Leaf 1
  • Salt as needed
  • To grind
    • 1 bunch Cilantro
    • 1/2 bunch Mint
    • 4-5 Green Chilies
    • 3 Shallots
    • 1/2 piece Ginger
    • 4-5 cloves Garlic
Method:
  • Marinate the chicken pieces with salt, pepper powder and lemon juice for at least half an hour before preparing the curry.
  • Make a smooth paste of all the ingredients under ‘to grind’. Try to avoid water while grinding or add as little as possible.
  • Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan and add the bay leaf and fennel seeds.
  • Add the finely chopped onions and fry till it is golden brown.
  • Add the ground paste and let it cook until the oil separates. This will take about 10 minutes.
  • Add the yogurt, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder and garam masala. Fry for about half a minute before adding the chicken.
  • After adding the chicken, saute it for at least 3 minutes.
  • Add required water, salt and bring it to a boil. Cover the pan and cook till the chicken is no more raw and oil starts separating from the gravy.
  • Add the heavy cream to the curry and mix well. Switch off and serve with naan, chapatti, pulav or white rice.

BAGARA KHANA(RICE)

Jul 14th, 2013 12:00 am

Bagara khana is a spiced rice delicacy that can be served with chicken korma or dalcha(lentils cooked with mutton).

Ingredients:
  • 4 cups Basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 Onion-sliced finely
  • 4 Green Cardamoms
  • 2 Cinnamon sticks
  • 2 Cloves
  • 1 1/4 tbsp Caraway seeds (Shah Zeera)
  • 4 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste.
  • Coriander and Mint leaves-finely chopped
Method:
  • Wash the rice and soak it in surplus water for about 1 hour.
  • Then in a large saucepan add oil at medium heat,add onion and fry till they are slightly golden in color.
  • Add ginger & garlic paste, fry for a minute.Then add water along with cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, caraway seeds and salt.
  • Let it come to boil,add the drained rice.Give it a stir and let the rice cook until it is done.
  •  Remove from the heat and serve hot.

BROWNIE TRIFLE

Jul 10th, 2013 12:00 am

Wishing you all a happy and blessed Ramadan.Ramadan Mubarak!! Let’s welcome Ramadan with sweet and tempting Brownie Trifle.It is usually served in one large trifle bowl.But it’s also fun to serve them as individual trifles with full of deliciousness.

Brownies

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup or 2 sticks Butter, melted
  • 1 cup unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 3 cups Sugar
  • 3 large Eggs
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 cup Bittersweet or Semisweet chocolate-finely chopped
  • Whipped Cream can
  • For Decoration-Fresh Strawberries,Blueberries,Chocolate Hazelnut Rolled Wafers
Method:
  • Preheat oven to 350Ëš. Grease square cake pan or line with parchment paper.
  • Make Brownie
    • In a medium bowl, stir together melted butter and cocoa and set aside.
    • In a large mixing bowl, beat sugar and eggs until fluffy, about five minutes. Slowly beat in the cocoa and butter mixture. Add flour, salt and extract; beat just until combined.
    • Spread half of the brownie mixture in the pan. Sprinkle with finely chopped chocolate.
    • Carefully spread the remaining brownie batter over the chocolate all the way to the edges.
    • Bake for 35-37 minutes. Cool completely. Cut into bite-size pieces or crush them.
  • In a large trifle bowl, layer brownies, whipped cream and strawberries. Repeat layers until full. Refrigerate for 1-2 hours before serving.
  • Garnish on top with whipped cream,strawberries and rolled wafers.

Enjoy :)

SKILLET LASAGNA

Jul 8th, 2013 12:00 am

Lasagna is an authentic Italian meal made with ground meat and tomato sauce.Traditional lasagna can take up to 2 hours to prepare.This recipe takes only 35 minutes as it cooks up in one big skillet,and no oven required.

The key ingredients..

Ingredients:

  • 1/2kg Ground Lamb or Beef
  • 1 jar Spaghetti sauce
  • 1-2/3 cups Water
  • 1/4 cup Zesty Italian Dressing-optional
  • 2 Green Bell peppers- chopped
  • 3 Cloves Garlic-minced
  • 12 Oven-ready Lasagna Noodle- broken into quarters
  • 1 cup Shredded Low-Moisture Part-Skim Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan and Ricotta cheese-optional

Method:

  • Add the ground lamb to the skillet or saucepan.Cook, stirring often and using a wooden spoon to break up the meat, about 3 to 5 minutes, or until it’s no longer pink.Then drain of the excess fat.
  • Next pour the spaghetti sauce and add bell peppers,minced garlic,Italian dressing;mix well. Bring to boil by adding water.
  • Drop in the lasagna noodles and cover the skillet with lid.
  • Cook on medium-low heat for 10 to 15 minutes or until noodles are tender, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat.
  • Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese or stir in half the mozzarella and parmesan, and 1/4 cup of the ricotta;if you like more cheese ; cover. Let stand 5 min. or until cheese is melted.
  • Remove and enjoy the hot lasagna.
