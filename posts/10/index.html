OREO MILKSHAKE

Oct 19th, 2012 12:00 am

No matter how you put Oreo Cookies and ice cream together,you know it’s going to be good.This cool,creamy and chocolaty treat is case in point:)

Ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup cold milk
  • 1/2 cup chocolate syrup
  • 2 cups Vanilla ice-cream
  • 14 Oreo Cookies
Method:
  • Crush 14 of the cookies coarsely;place in blend container.
  • Add milk,vanilla ice cream and chocolate syrup.
  • Cover and blend until smooth.
  • Pour into glass.Top it with scoop of ice cream and a cookie.
  • Serve immediately.

DREAMWHIP FRUIT TRIFLE

Oct 15th, 2012 12:00 am

A light, fresh, and cool dessert.Easy Recipe,Perfect for Parties.

Here is the image of the Dreamwhip whipped topping mix packet

Ingredients:
  • 2 Envelopes Dream whip Whipped topping mix
  • 1 tin Nestle cream
  • 1 tin Fruit Cocktail or fresh fruits of your choice
  • 10 Bread slices
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1 cup Water
Method:
  • Beat the dream whip whipped topping mix and nestle cream and set aside.
  • Prepare the Sugar syrup-Take a saucepan and boil the water and sugar until the sugar dissolves completely and keep aside.
  • Take 10 slices of bread and cut all the brown sides of bread and cut into small cubes.
  • Now dip each bread cube into the sugar syrup and place it in the flat glass dish and then pour a layer of beaten dreamwhip on bread and then put some fruit cocktail.
  • Next form the second layer by placing the bread cubes dipped in sugar syrup ,then pour the dreamwhip and then top it with fruit cocktails.
  • Refrigerate for 4-5 hours and Serve chilled.
  • Enjoy the dessert:)

KHEEMA

Oct 11th, 2012 12:00 am

Kheema or mince can be made with any meat you prefer. It is extremely versatile.Kheema tastes delicious with Chapatis or Parathas.

Ingredients:
  • Minced Meat 1/2 kg
  • Potatoes 2,cubed
  • Onions 2, large (thinly sliced)
  • Tomatoes 3, medium (roughly sliced)
  • Ginger and Garlic Paste: 1 1/2 tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Red Chilli Powder 1tbsp  or as to taste
  • Turmeric Powder 1tsp
  • Coriander Powder 1 1/2 tsp
  • Tomato Ketchup 2 tbsp
  • Green Cardamom 3-4
  • Cloves 4
  • Green Chillies 3-4, pricked
  • Coriander Leaves 1/2 bunch, finely chopped
  • Oil: 1 cup
Method:
  • In a deep cooking pan, heat oil and fry thinly sliced onion till it gets soft and transparent.
  • Stir in the cloves, green cardamoms along with ginger and ginger pastes. Now fry all till onions turn light golden brown.
  • Add in minced meat, salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder and coriander powder. Stir fry till minced meat loses it’s pink color. Add enough water that covers minced meat. Put on medium heat and cook till minced meat is half done.
  • Now add potatoes and tomatoes. If required, then add a little more water so that potatoes gets cooked. Stir, taste and fix salt at this point. Cover and cook till potatoes are almost done.
  • Now add in tomato ketchup and green chillies. Stir and cover on medium heat so that water is well absorbed and potatoes are nicely cooked. Lastly, stir in the coriander leaves and switch off the flame.
  • Serve hot with Paratha or Chapati or Naan.

CINNAMON FRENCH TOAST

Oct 4th, 2012 12:00 am

Cinnamon French Toast is the best morning breakfast.For this you can use the regular bread or the cinnamon bread.I used the regular bread. Very easy and energy toast,you have to try it.

Ingredients:
  • Bread Slices-4
  •  1/2 cup Milk
  • Eggs 3
  • Salt 1/4tsp
  • Vanilla Essence 1tsp-optional
  • Cinnamon powder 1tbsp
  • Butter 1tbsp
  • Pancake syrup or Honey
  • Whipped Cream
  • Some fruits-Banana and strawberries
Method:
    • In a bowl,whisk together the milk,eggs,salt,cinnamon powder and vanilla essence.
    • Dip a slice of bread into the mixture covering both sides.
    • In a non stick pan,melt butter and place slices of bread at a time into the pan.
    • Cook until golden brown approximately 2 minutes per side and then remove from pan.
    • Drizzle some pancake syrup or honey and whipped cream on top and serve with fruits.

Enjoy :)

TOMATO MUTTON

Oct 2nd, 2012 12:00 am

A mild mutton curry, with a fragrant tomato gravy.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 kg Mutton
  • 1 Onion -finely chopped
  • 2 Tomatoes-finely chopped
  • 1tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1tbsp Red chilli powder
  • 1/4tbsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 Green chillies
  • 1tbsp lemon juice-optional
  • Salt to taste
  • 4tbsp oil
  • Coriander leaves-finely chopped
  • Mint leaves-finely chopped
Method:
  • Wash and marinate the mutton with ginger-garlic paste,salt,red chilli powder,turmeric powder and keep aside.
  • Take a heavy bottomed pan,add oil at medium heat and add onion;fry till they are slightly golden in color.
  • Add the marinated mutton and mix well and let it cook for few minutes.
  • Now add the chopped tomatoes and green chillies and let it cook till it gets smooth and mushy.
  • Now add water and cook until the mutton is tender.
  • Then leave it on for low heat till the thin layer of oil appears on the bottom surface and cover the pan and leave till the gravy is thick.
  • Add coriander and mint leaves.
  • Remove from heat and serve with parathas and plain rice.

CHICKEN BIRYANI-II

Oct 1st, 2012 12:00 am

This is a good recipe for a Chicken Biryani,a popular spiced Indian rice.

My hubby loved it and complimented me by saying it tasted like restaurant biryani..:).So do try out.

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2kg Chicken
  • 4 cups Basmati Rice
  • 3 large Onions sliced
  • 2 tbsp Ghee/1 cup Oil
  • 1 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  • 1 tbsp Green chilli paste
  • 1 tsp Ajinomoto
  • 1 tbsp Biryani Masala-grind cinnamon,cloves,cardamon and shahzeera
  • Shaan Biryani Packet-for extra taste
  • Coriander-finely chopped
  • Mint leaves-finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Saffron dissolved in hot water
  • For Rice
    • 1 tbsp Shahzeera/Caraway seeds
    • 4 Black Peppercorns
    • 4 Green Cardamons
    • 2 Cinnamon Sticks
    • 2 Cloves
    • Salt to taste
Method:
    • In a large sauce pan at medium heat ,add 2tbsp ghee,add onions and fry till golden brown and keep aside.
    • In a large bowl,add the chicken and marinate with ginger-garlic paste,red chilli powder,turmeric powder,garam masala powder,ajinomoto,green chilli paste,,biryani masala,yogurt,fried onions,finely chopped coriander and mint leaves,salt and 3tbsp oil.
    • Mix the chicken well and cover and leave it for 3hours or more.
    • Next wash the rice and soak it in surlpus water for about 1/2 hour.
    • Meanwhile,in a large cooking pot or rice cooker;pour in water along with cloves,cardamon,cinnamon sticks,shazeera and salt.
    • Let it come to boil,add the drained rice.Give it a stir and let the rice cook until it is half done,2-3 minutes.Drain the rice.
    • Now take a large vessel/non stick vessel;add the marinated chicken.Spread the drained cooked rice over the chicken.
    • Spread evenly the fried onions and saffron color.
    • Then cover the vessel lid with newspaper or aluminium foil;and place a heavy weighted vessel over it so that no steam can escape.
    • Place the biryani vessel on stove and cook on low flame.
    • Allow to cook for 10-15 mins.
    • Serve the delicious biryani with dahi chutney and mirch salan.

COLD COFFEE

Sep 29th, 2012 12:00 am

Well I am not much in love with hot coffee but I enjoy cold coffee more.So here goes the cooling and refreshing coffee drink.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups Vanilla Ice cream
  • 1/2 cup Chilled Milk
  • Sugar to taste
  • 1tbsp Coffee
  • 1tbsp Cream-optional
  • Ice cubes 2
Method:
    • In a blender, blend 2cups of vanilla ice cream,chilled milk,sugar,coffee and ice cubes until frothy and creamy.
    • Now take a glass pour the cold coffee mixture and then you can add 1 scoop of ice cream or cream on top.
    • Cold Coffee is ready to enjoy.

Enjoy :)

Note:If you don’t like vanilla ice-cream,you can make the coffee by skipping the ice-cream or can use any other flavored ice-cream.

KABSA -I

Sep 27th, 2012 12:00 am

Kabsa is a tradional dish of Saudi Arabia.There are different ways to prepare but this is easiest way to prepare.It’s my all time favorite and this taste great when served with chicken.

Ingredients:
  • 2cups Basmati Rice
  • 3cups water
  • 4 Green cardamons
  • 4 Cloves
  • 4 Black pepper corns
  • 1 Chicken cube stock
  • 2 Carrots grated
  • 10 Raisins
  • Oil
Method:
  • Put oil in a pan and add cloves,cardamons,black pepper corns and fry for 2-3 minutes on medium heat.
  • Add chicken cube and cook for 2 minutes.
  • Now add rice and water and cook the rice with lid on.
  • In a separate pan,add oil and fry the grated carrots for 3minutes and keep aside.And in the same oil fry the raisins for 2 minutes.
  • After the rice has been cooked,add the fried carrots and raisins.
  • Serve rice with tomato sauce.

VEG SCHEZWAN NOODLES

Sep 24th, 2012 12:00 am

Veg Schezwan Noodles are spicy,tasty and delicious Chinese dish,ready in few minutes.The easy recipe of schezwan veg noodles is here.

Ingredients:
  • 1 packet Spagethi
  • 2tbsp home made Schezwan sauce/Ching’s Secret Schezwan sauce
  • 1tbsp Soya sauce
  • 1 Carrot diced
  • 1 Green bell pepper sliced
  • 1 Red bell pepper sliced
  • 1 Onion sliced
  • 1 Spring onion chopped
  • 4-5 French beans diced-optional
  • 1/4 medium Cabbage ,shredded
  • 1tbsp Vinegar
  • Minced ginger and garlic
  • 2tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
Method:
  • Boil the water with little salt and then add the spagethi and boil them.
  • Once the noodles are done,drain the water and add sesame oil to keep the noodles soft and separate.
  • Cut all the veggies into fine julience.
  • Heat oil in frying pan.Add the minced ginger and garlic.
  • Now add all the vegetables and cook on high heat.
  • Add vinger,soya sauce and salt and stir well.
  • Add noodles and mix well
  • Serve hot and garnish with spring onions.
