MICROWAVE PINEAPPLE PASTRY

Dec 25th, 2012 12:00 am

Wishing you all a very Happy Christmas!!.Christmas celebration is all about cakes,pastries and cookies.Enjoy your Christmas with easy and hassle free ending Microwave Pineapple Pastry.

Cake frosted with Cool whip.

Cake fosted with buttercream frosting.

Ingredients:
  • 2tbsp Butter
  • 2tbsp Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Warm Milk
  • 1/2tsp Vanilla essence
  • 1 cup All Purpose Flour(Maida)
  • 1/2tsp Baking powder
  • 1/2tsp Baking Soda
  • Tinned Pineapple chunks
  • 1 pack of Cool Whip or Betty Crocker Whipped Butter Cream Frosting-(whipped into creamy)
Method:
    • Take butter and cream cheese in mixing bowl.To that add sugar and whisk well until it turns out creamy.
    • Slowly add warm milk and make that in to thin paste.Now add vanilla essence and mix well.
    • Sieve maida in to wet mixture along with baking powder and baking soda.Mix very well.
    • Pour the mixture in to greased 8*8 microwave safe Baking tray.Microwave over high power for 4 minutes.
    • After cake is done allow it to cool down for 10 minutes.Then transfer that on to cooling rack.
    • After cake cools down divide that in to two equal size sponge sheets.
    • Take one of the sponge sheet.Apply pineapple juice over that.Apply a layer of cool whip over it.
    • Place few chunks of pineapple over cream layer.
    • Now put another sponge sheet on the top of previous sponge sheet.Now cover the whole cake with cool whip.
    • Put some pineapple pieces on the top of cake and top that up with cherries or strawberries.
    • Slice it in to small pastry cakes!

Enjoy :)

BUTTER CHICKEN

Dec 18th, 2012 12:00 am

Butter chicken (or murgh makhani) is part of Indian cuisine, popular in countries all over the world.This rich, buttery, creamy chicken is perfect for parties and family gatherings.

Ingredients:
For Marination:
  • Boneless Chicken
  • 1/4 cup Yoghurt
  • 1tbsp Ginger and garlic paste
  • 1tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1/4tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1tbsp Red chilly powder
  • 1/2tsp Garam masala Powder
  • For Gravy:
    • 1tbsp Butter
    • Onion-2
    • Tomato-2
    • 1tsp Ginger and garlic paste
    • 1tsp Chilly powder
    • Almond/Cashew nut-a handful (soaked in water for 15 minutes)
    • Cardamom-2
    • Cloves-2
    • Cinnamon-1stick
    • Pepper corns-1tsp
    • Coriander leaves (chopped)
    • 1/2tsp Sugar
    • 2tbsp Fresh cream
    Method:
    • Marinate chicken with turmeric powder, chilly powder, garam masala, yoghurt ,ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice and salt.Keep it aside for an hour.
    • Heat 1tbsp butter in a pan and saute onions unil translucent.Add ginger garlic paste and saute well another 2 minutes.Add chilly powder and fry for a minute.Now add tomatoes and saute until tomatoes turn soft.Add almonds /cashew nuts and remove from heat.Cool completely and grind it into a smooth paste.
    • Heat 1 tbsp butter in a pan or pressure cooker and fry cardamom ,cloves,cinnamon and pepper powder.Add ground paste,3/4 cup water,sugar and salt.
    • When it starts boiling add marinated chicken and coriander leaves.
    • Cover and cook on medium heat or pressure cook for one whistle,until desired consistency is reached.
    • Switch off the flame and finally add fresh cream.Mix well
    • Garnish with fresh cream and coriander leaves.
    • Serve with chapathi,rice,parathas,or naan.

CHOCOLATE BISCUIT PUDDING

Dec 14th, 2012 12:00 am

Chocolate biscuit pudding- a deliciously rich dessert perfect for chocaholics!

Ingredients:
  • 500ml milk
  • half tin condensed milk
  • 1 tin nestle cream
  • 3tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 Hide and Seek biscuit packets or any biscuits of your choice
  • Few crushed almonds and pistachios for garnishing
  • Some chocolates
Method:
    • Take a pot and pour the milk in it along with condensed milk, cocoa powder, cream and vanilla essence.
    • Bring it to boil and cook it properly.. keep cooking it until it becomes thick.. like that of the consistency of the custard.. not too thin but not too thick either, turn the stove off and take the pot off the stove.
    • Now take a serving dish and layer the biscuits
    • Next, pour half the hot pudding onto the layered biscuits
    • Sprinkle some almonds and pistachios over this
    • Keep continuing these layers and that’s it!
    • refrigerate it until well chilled and serve it with vanilla ice-cream or whipped cream. —

Enjoy :)

PEA PULAO

Dec 9th, 2012 12:00 am

Pea Pulao is also known as Yellow Rice. It is a great alternative to plain boiled rice and accompaniment for any main course, be it vegetarian or meat-based. 

Ingredients:
  • 2tbsps oil/ghee
  • 1 Onion- chopped finely
  • 1cup Green peas
  • 2cups Basmati rice
  • 3cups Water
  • 1/2tsp Turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup warm water
Method:
  • Wash the Basmati rice well in running water and keep aside to soak for 30 minutes.
  • Heat the oil in a deep heavy-bottomed pan. When it is hot add the onions and cook till soft.
  • Add the peas and stir well.Add the rice and fry for 2 minutes.
  • Add the 3 cups of water, turmeric and salt to taste to the rice and set it up to boil on a medium flame.
  • Once the water comes to a boil, reduce the flame to a simmer and cover the pan.
  • Cook till the water seems to have almost disappeared - tiny holes will form on the surface of the rice.
  • Add the 1/2 cup of warm water all over the top of the rice and cover the pan again. Simmer for another 5-7 minutes and turn off the fire.
  • Allow the rice to sit for 10 minutes and serve.

LIVER MASALA(KALEJI MASALA)

Dec 3rd, 2012 12:00 am

Liver masala recipe is one of the spicy side dish. In local words it is also called as Khaleji masala.Liver is great for blood formation.

Ingredients:
  • Liver 1/2 kg,
  • Onion 1 large,
  • Ginger paste 1 tsp,
  • Red chilli powder 1/2 tsp,
  • Corriander powder 1 tsp,
  • Turmeric 1/2 tsp,
  • Corriander leaves, green chilies and garam masala for garnishing,
  • Dry Fenugreek leaves 1/2 tsp
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste,
  • Oil 1/4 cup
  • Yogurt-optional
Method:
  • Slice onions and fry them till golden brown and add curry leaves.
  • Add the liver pieces(cut into small cubes), ginger/garlic paste, red chilli powder, corriander powder,turmeric and salt.
  • Add some water if needed and bring to boil.Simmer till done.
  • Add some yogurt and fenugreek leaves at the end and stir it on medium high until it leaves oil.
  • Sprinkle corriander leaves, sliced green chilies and garam masala.
  • Serve hot with naan or roti.

TRIPLE BERRY SMOOTHIE

Nov 27th, 2012 12:00 am

Berries are nutritious and low in calories.I have used three types of berries in this smoothie but you can try this with any berries that you have on hand. Start your day with a glass of smoothie and add berries to your diet for a healthy life style.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup each Fresh Strawberries, Blackberries and Raspberries
  • 1/2 cup Low fat flavored yogurt or 1/2cup vanilla ice cream
  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 1/2 cup of ice cubes
  • 1 cup of milk
Method:
    • In a blender, add all the berries along with ice cubes, half of the milk, honey and yogurt or ice cream and blend until smooth.
    • Add the rest of the milk if it is too thick. Pour it in a tall glass and enjoy your smoothie.
    • You can garnish with fresh fruits and mint leaves.

Enjoy :)

UPMA

Nov 19th, 2012 12:00 am

Here is the super simple upma recipe that you can have for breakfast. It’s very delicious and easy to make.

Ingredients:
  • 1 tbsp Channa dal
  • 2 tbsp Peanuts
  • 1 Onion small -chopped
  • 1 Tomato -chopped(optinal)
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 8 Curry leaves
  • 2 Green chillies-sliced
  • Hing -a pinch
  • 1 Ginger slice-chopped or 1 tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Oil or Butter
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup Sooji/Semolina/Upma Rava
  • 2 cups Water
Method:
  • In a pan add oil,add mustard seeds,cumin seeds once they splutter add channa dal ,peanuts and saute till they are cooked.
  • Now add chopped ginger,onions,chillies, curry leaves, hing,salt,turmeric powder and saute them.Next add water and bring it to boil.
  • Next add upma rawa/semolina/sooji slowly stirring continously so to avoid lumps and mix well.When all the moisture is absorbed cover for 5 minutes and serve hot with chutney or pickle.

GULAB JAMUN-Diwali Special

Nov 13th, 2012 12:00 am

Wishing you all a very Happy and Safe Diwali!!!May this festival fill with light and fun :).Here is the special diwali treat for all.Enjoy!!

Gulab jamuns are an Indian version of donuts immersed in the warm sweet syrup. Served as a dessert, this is a staple in most parts of India.

Ingredients:
  • 4 tbsp Nido or any other powder milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder (level/plain)
  • 2 tsp Semolina (sooji)
  • 1 medium Egg
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 2 cups Water
  • 4 coarsely Grounded Cardamom seeds
  • few sliced Almonds and Pistachio
  • Oil for deep-frying
Method:
  • Prepare Sugar Syrup:
    • In a large pan, add water, sugar, and ground cardamom seeds and bring it to a boil.
    • Stir the syrup until the sugar is dissolved.Set the syrup aside.
  • Make Gulab Jamun:
    • In a bowl, mix milk powder,semolina and baking powder.Add oil and egg and mix well.
    • Let the dough sit for a few minutes. The dough should be soft. Add more milk powder to the dough if you are adding large egg.
    • Knead the dough. Grease your hands with oil before working with the dough.
  • Divide the dough into 10 to 14 equal portions and roll them into round balls.
  • Heat the oil in a frying pan on medium heat. The frying pan should have at least 1 inch of oil. To test if the oil is the right temperature, place a small piece of dough into the oil; it should take a minute to rise. If dough rises faster, oil is too hot; if dough just sits without rising, oil is not hot enough.
  • Place the gulab jamuns in the frying pan. The gulab jamuns will expand in double the volume, so give them enough space.
  • It should take about 7 minutes to fry the gulab jamuns. While frying keep rolling the gulab jamuns around so they are evenly browned. Fry until the gulab jamuns become dark brown.
  • Let the gulab jamuns cool off for a few minutes before placing in the hot syrup.
  • The gulab jamuns should sit in the hot syrup for few minutes prior to serving.

CARAMEL PUDDING

I made these delightful puddings from a ready made carton mix today as wanted to prepare something quick.This makes a beautiful and quick dessert. This dessert always remind me of my sweet little cousin sis who calls me by the name Pudding di :).It’s kind of long story why she kept my name pudding..lol

This is how the pudding packet looks like..

Ingredients:
  • Milk 2cups
  • Caramel pudding powder packet
  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • Cardamom pods
Method:
  • Boil the milk (as per the carton instructions),while boiling add a stick of cinnamon and a couple of cardamom pods for better taste.
  • Empty the caramel sauce (that comes in the box) in a dish and keep aside.

  • Make sure you discard the cinnamon stick and cardamom pods first though! Serve chilled! )
  • Cool and refrigerate for few hours atleast 1hour before serving.
