FRENCH BEANS and POTATO CURRY

Jul 2nd, 2013 12:00 am

Quick and easy curry Of French Beans With Potato that goes well with chapati and rice.French beans are a great source of carbohydrates and dietary fibers. I cook this just very often as it is simple.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 kg French Beans
  • 1 Potato
  • 1 Onion-finely chopped
  • 1 Tomato-finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Red chili powder
  • 1/4 tbsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil
  • Coriander leaves-finely chopped
  • Mint leaves-finely chopped
Method:
  • Wash and cut potato and French beans into cubes ,or in a shape you want to cut it.
  • In a large non-stick saucepan or pressure cooker add oil at medium heat, add onions and fry till they change color to golden brown.
  • Add ginger and garlic paste,fry for 1 minute.And then add chopped tomato, red chili powder,turmeric powder,salt and cook till tomato are soft and mushy.
  • Next add the chopped green beans, potato and 1/2 cup of water as per the requirement and cook well.
  • Do not cook on a high heat,simmer heat and add the coriander leaves & mint leaves. Cover the pan and leave for 15-20 minutes until the green beans and potato are soft & tender.
  • When done add the coriander powder and cumin powder and stir for 5 minutes.
  • Remove from the heat and serve with plain rice or chapati.

BANANA BREAD

Jun 29th, 2013 12:00 am

Moist and delicious Banana bread made with ripe bananas.So if you find ripe bananas in your kitchen don’t toss them away instead bake banana bread. 

Ingredients:
  • 4 ripe Bananas
  • 1/2 cup Raisins
  • 1/2 cup Walnuts or Silvered Almonds
  • 1/2 cup Chocolate chips-optional
  • 2 Eggs
  • A pinch of Salt
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp Baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  • Butter
  • 1 1/2 cup of All Purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup of Sugar
Method:
  • Preheat oven at 350*F/ 180*C.
  • Break and mash the ripe bananas in a bowl and set aside.
  • Next melt the butter and give a quick stir so it is in liquid paste form.To the butter add sugar,eggs,vanilla extract and mix it.And then add the mashed bananas and mix well.
  • Sieve all purpose flour,baking powder,baking soda and salt into the bowl and mix it gently.
  • Next drop the walnuts,almonds and raisins.You can replace the dry fruits with chocolate chips or cocoa powder.Don’t over mix the batter or it will end up into a hard bread.
  • Pour the batter into a loaf tin lined with parchment paper and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  • Once it is baked,leave the baked banana to cool at room temperature for 10-12 minutes.
  • Peel of the parchment paper and slice it using bread knife.
  • Enjoy the fresh and moist banana bread.

KIDNEY BEANS(RAJMA CURRY)

Jun 26th, 2013 12:00 am

Kidney beans are some of the healthiest most nutritious beans to have in one’s diet. They are full of proteins. I normally prepare them in this way and can be served with rice.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup of Kidney beans, washed then soaked overnight
  • 3 big fresh Tomatoes
  • 3-4 leaves Methi/Fenugreek
  • Oil
  • 1 big Onion-finely chopped or grated
  • 1 tbsp Tomato paste
  • ¼ tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • ½ tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • Coriander leaves -chopped
  • 1-2 Green chilies
  • Salt
Method:
  • Drain the kidney beans, they will have expanded to about 2-3 times the original volume.
  • Blend the tomatoes with their skin, along with the coriander leaves and the green chilies to a smooth paste.
  • Heat the oil in a pressure cooker, add the fenugreek leaves, followed by the garlic and ginger paste. Stir for a minute, then add the onions. Fry until they are translucent. Add the tomato paste and the spices (cumin, turmeric, coriander powders) and let these fry for a few minutes on low heat.
  • Then add the blended tomatoes and let this cook/simmer until the gravy thickens and the raw tomato smell diminishes.
  • Then add the kidney beans plus some salt and stir together for a few minutes.
  • Finally add about 3-4 cups water and let it come to a boil. Cover the pressure cooker, and cook on medium heat for about 10-12 minutes.
  • Once the pressure/steam is released, open the pressure cooker and check the beans. If they are done, then sprinkle with a bit of coriander leaves and serve. If not yet, then cover and cook for another 2-3 minutes. This depends on the type of kidney beans you have, some take longer than others.
  • If you prefer more gravy with your beans, you can add some water after the kidney beans are done and let it simmer for a few minutes. You can mash some of the kidney beans to thicken the gravy to a lovely consistency.
  • Serve the beans with simple boiled/steamed rice or with chapatis/parathas,as preferred!

CREAMY PUDDING

Jun 26th, 2013 12:00 am

Delicious creamy pudding can be a enjoyed as a dessert with in no time.Very easy and quick to prepare. I used Dr.Oetker pudding powder in vanilla flavor to make the pudding.

Ingredients:
  • 1 liter milk whole milk or full cream milk
  • 1 glass Sugar
  • 1/2 glass Cold Water
  • 2 packs of Pudding powder(Original Vanilla)
Method:
  • Take a pot and boil your milk. When ur milk started to boil, keep on low heat and add suger.Let it boil for 1 or 2 minutes until the sugar is dissolved with milk.
  • Now mix the pudding powder with cold water very well in a separate bowl.
  • Add pudding powder mixture which u mixed with cold water to the boiled milk on low heat . While adding pudding powder, keep stirring as well and dont boil for long minutes.
  • After 2 mins it will be ready.Now pour the pudding into serving dish and don’t move it from that place for a while.
  • After a while when it becomes cold, decorate in any way u want and then put in the refrigerator so as to taste better.
  • Serve chilled and enjoy.

MAC and CHEESE

May 31st, 2013 12:00 am

Easy cheesy Mac and Cheese, goodness!This dish is quick, easy, and contains only natural, whole ingredients.

Ingredients:
  • 12 oz Evaporated milk or 1 1/2cups of Milk
  • 1/2-1 cup milk (separate from milk above)
  • 2 cups of Mini shells pasta [pasta size indicates cook style + time - see note below
  • 1 cup of cooked Veggies(peas,carrots,broccoli or your choice)
  • 1/4 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp dried Parsley
  • 1/4 tsp Salt as per taste
  • a sprinkle of mustard powder-optional
  • freshly ground Black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup sharp Cheddar Cheese, freshly grated
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
Method:
  • Cook or re-heat your veggies.You can steam,saute or even microwave the veggies or can use frozen veggies.And next grate your cheeses and keep aside.
  • In a medium-sized pot add pasta and 1/2 cup milk and season with 1/4 tsp of salt, 1/4 tsp of garlic powder and black pepper.Stir often to prevent the noodles from sticking and the milk from burning.
  • Depending on the size of your noodles, you may need to add an extra 1/2 cup of milk to help them cook.
  • Cook and stir constantly.In about 10-15 minutes or so the noodles will be fully cooked and the milk will have thickened into a creamy sauce.Now turn the heat to the lowest setting.
  • Now add the cheddar and parmesan cheeses.Use the little bit of milk to adjust the sauce to your desired thickness and even add a little extra cheese to taste.
  • Add a pinch of mustard powder for a color/flavor boost as well as an extra pinch of garlic powder.Add salt and pepper to taste if needed.
  • Stir in your steamed/cooked/frozen veggies after adding your cheeses and garnish with a sprinkle of parsley if desired.
  • You can also top it with red pepper flakes for a pretty garnish that delivers a little kick of heat.

CHICKEN CHICK PEA CURRY(MURGH CHOLE)

May 28th, 2013 12:00 am

Chicken-Chole – what a nice combination.I stopped eavesdropping and started thinking of the recipe and this is how I made it.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup Chickpeas (Chole) – soaked in water overnight
  • Chicken breast – cleaned, cut in cubes
  • 1 medium Onion – grind to paste
  • 1 medium Tomato – grind to paste
  • 1/2 tbsp Cumin seeds
  • 2 whole Black peppers and Cloves
  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • 2 Cardamoms
  • 1 tbsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp Coriander powder(Dhaniya powder)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger and garlic paste
  • a pinch of Turmeric powder
  • Salt per taste
  • 1 tbsp Lime juice
  • Oil.
  • 1/2 cup Warm Water
Method:
  • First boil the soaked chickpeas or can use canned chickpeas.
  • Heat oil in a big wok or crockpot.Add in cumin seeds, black peppers, cloves, cinnamon stick, and cardamom.Let it cook for about 2-3 minutes.
  • Add in onion paste and cook till golden brown.
  • Add in tomato paste and cook till oil separates.
  • Next add in red chili powder,turmeric dhaniya powder, salt, lime juice.Cook till oil separates.
  • Now add in cubed chicken and boiled chickpeas.Let it cook till oil separates and chicken is fully cooked.
  • You may add warm water to get the consistency of a gravy.
  • Serve hot with roti or plain rice.

LAMB POTATO SPINACH CURRY(ALOO PALAK GOSHT)

May 7th, 2013 12:00 am

Spinach is one of my favorite veggie dishes because it is full of nutrients and health benefits.There are two ways to prepare ,non-vegetarian can enjoy aloo palak gosht and vegetarian,discard the mutton and enjoy aloo palak curry.

Ingredients:
  • 1 big bunch of Spinach
  • Mutton pieces
  • 1 medium Onion, grated or chopped finely
  • 1 large Potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 Tomato, chopped
  • 1-2 Green chillies
  • 1 tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
  • pinch of Turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Coriander for garnishing
Method:
  • First wash and marinate the mutton pieces with ginger and garlic paste,salt,turmeric powder and red chilli powder for 30 minutes.
  • Take your bunch of fresh spinach.Wash/clean and get the leaves off the stems. Discard stems and drain the leaves then chop them into very thin slices.
  • Heat some oil in a pan,add the chopped onion and fry to a nice light brown, then add a green chili with slits and then the marinated mutton pieces.
  • Cook for few minutes and then add the chopped tomatoes and sprinkle coriander powder. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and mushy and you can see the oil being released from the sides.
  • Add the cubed potatoes at this point so they can absorb the flavors properly.Next add water and bring the mutton and potatoes to boil.
  • Next add the spinach, give it a stir then cover and let this cook, giving it an occasional stir every few minutes.
  • The spinach will reduce drastically in amount. Do not cook it for too long, you will lose all the nutrients.You can add water if it get dries completely.
  • Once it’s done to your liking,garnish with coriander and serve.

INDIAN SPICY MAGGIE NOODLES

May 7th, 2013 12:00 am

Healthy and easy noodles recipe with a mixture of spices and veggies.

Ingredients:
  • Maggie Noodles 1 packet
  • 1 Onion medium,sliced
  • 1/2 Tomato chopped
  • Carrots and Green peas
  • 1 Green and Red bell pepper,cut into 1 inch long pieces
  • 2 Green chillies chopped
  • Ginger and Garlic grated or chopped
  • 1 tbsp Red chilli powder
  • 2 tbsp Soya sauce
  • Oil
  • 2 cups Water
  • Salt to taste
Method:
  • Take a pan,add little oil and sliced onion.Cook till the onions are translucent.
  • Next add the veggies,green and red bell pepper.Let it cook with onions for 5 minutes.
  • Add carrots and peas,chopped green chilies,grated ginger and garlic and tomatoes.Cook all the veggies.
  • Now add salt,red chili powder and soya sauce.Mix well and cook on medium heat.
  • Next add water and uncooked maggie into the pan.Cover and cook till the noodles are done.
  • Serve hot and garnish with coriander leaves.Enjoy with ketchup.:)

CHICKEN 65

Apr 28th, 2013 12:00 am

The weather is so awesome out here today that I have been having such crazy cravings. Hot and spicy Chicken 65 is what is running on my mind right now!…Mmmmm..Just Yummm!!

Ingredients:
  • For Marination:
    • 500gm boneless Chicken breast (cut into small cubes)
    • 2 tsps Corn flour
    • 1 tsp Salt
    • 1 Egg-lightly beaten
    • 1/2tsp Black pepper powder
    • 1tsp. Ginger and Garlic paste
    • Oil for frying
    • For Preparation:
      • 1/2tsp Cumin seeds
      • 2tbsps Oil
      • 1tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
      • 1/2tsp Black pepper powder
      • 1/2tsp Cumin powder
      • Salt to taste
      • 1tsp Red chili powder
      • 2tbsps Chili garlic sauce
      • Mint and Coriander leaves-freshly choed
      • 3 Green chilies-chopped
      • few Curry leaves
      • 1tsp Garam masala
      • Water
      • few drops red food color
      • 1/4 cup of thick plain yoghurt -whipped
      • Ketchup-if you want dark red color.
      • Spring onions-sliced for garnish
    Method:
    • In a bowl mix together chicken, salt, ginger and garlic paste, black pepper, corn flour, and egg.Let it marinate for half an hour.
    • Heat oil for deep frying in a deep pan. Lower the flame, and add in the chicken pieces slowly. (Do not over crowd the pan). Fry for not more than 3 minutes. Do not over-fry, it will result in hard chicken. Remove and set aside to drain on kitchen towels.
    • Take another pan, add oil. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add in ginger, garlic, green chilies, curry leaves and fry for a minute. Next add black pepper powder, cumin powder, chili powder, and salt to taste. Then add chili garlic paste, chopped mint, chopped coriander leaves, garam masala and fry for about 1-2 minutes on medium flame.
    • Add little water (a few tbsps.) and cook for a minute on medium flame. Add red food color, mix well and throw in the fried chicken pieces. Mix them well so that the spices coat nicely. Add yoghurt and mix well to coat the chicken. Increase the flame to high and cook till the yoghurt is absorbed fully. Taste and adjust salt/spices. Turn off the heat.
    • You can garnish with sliced onions, lemon,salad and serve hot. Goes well as a side dish for fried rice, fries etc.
