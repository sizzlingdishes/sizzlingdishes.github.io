PURI or POORI RECIPE-FRIED BREAD

Apr 22nd, 2013 12:00 am

Puris are fried wheat bread, very common in Indian cuisine served for breakfast. It is a very nice accompaniment to curries as well as snacks.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups All Purpose Flour or Maida
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Ghee or Oil
  • 1 tsp Baking powder or baking soda
  • 1 cup Water (or as needed to make a pliable dough)
  • Oil-for frying
Method:
  • Mix together the flour, baking powder and salt thoroughly.
  • Add water little by little to the dough .Knead for 15-20 minutes or until it comes together into a pliable dough. You can also add 1 tbsp hot oil or ghee to the dough. (that you are heating for deep frying).The dough should not be sticky (only very lightly sticky) or feel dry in your hands.
  • Do not let the dough sit as it soaks up lots of oil while frying. Hence use the dough immediately to make the puris.Some say that dough must rest in a humid place like oven for atleast an hour.
  • Make small balls out of it.Roll it out into small circles with thin-medium thickness using rolling pin. Don’t make it too thin else it will come out crispy. Let the puris be a little thick so that they will puff up.
  • Repeat for all dough.Heat oil for deep frying.The oil should be hot but not too hot that it smokes.
  • Drop a small piece of dough into the oil. The dough should fry up immediately and come to the surface. You know the oil is not hot enough when the puri does not puff up, is flat and instead is also soaking up oil. Now drop the puri into the oil.
  • While the puri is slowly coming to the surface,keep tapping slowly on the puri with a ladle so that it would puff up. When one side changes color, turn it over and let the other side cook. Make sure you turn the puri just once.
  • Place it on paper towel to drain off any oil. Serve it with side dish of your choice. The famous combo is with Potato Masala and also Chole (chickpeas curry).

CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

Mar 14th, 2013 12:00 am

Chocolate Chip Cookies my favorite cookies ,are easy to prepare and are crisp outer and soft chewy inside.Home-baked cookies are favourite treat for everyone.I baked this cookies for my friend and she loved it!!

Ingredients:
  • 2 1/4 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) Butter, softened at room temp (or use 1 stick butter and 1 stick margarine.)
  • 3/4 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 2 cups (12 oz. package) Nestle Toll House Semi-Sweet Chocolate Morsels
  • 1 cup chopped Nuts (optional)
Method:
  • Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar in a mixing bowl until creamy.
  • Take a large mixing bowl and beat the eggs.To this add the sugar mixture and vanilla essence and whisk well.
  • Gradually beat in the sift flour,baking soda and salt till it becomes like dough.
  • Now add in chocolate morsels and nuts and stir it.
  • Drop and slightly press the cookie dough onto the baking tray lined with parchment paper by rounded tablespoons or an ice cream scoop,so that cookies are in circles for uniform sizing.
  • Bake for 9 to 12 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on baking tray for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
  • Transfer cookies to a nice serving plate when they have cooled.Enjoy the yummy cookies.

WHITE CHICKEN KORMA

Mar 10th, 2013 12:00 am

Enjoy the simple, mild chicken korma with naan or rice. 

Ingredients:
  • 2 boneless Chicken breasts, cubed or Whole Chicken
  • 1/2 tsp whole Cumin seeds
  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • 2 Cardamom pods
  • 1 medium Onion, chopped finely
  • 1 tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup Sour cream or Yoghurt
  • 1/2 cup Coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 Green chilli
  • Coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1 tsp Cornflour
  • Salt to taste
  • juice of half lemon
  • Almonds for garnishing (optional)
Method:
  • Marinate the chicken in the following spices-1 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp red chilli flakes or green chillies, 1/2 tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp ginger and garlic paste, 1 tbsp oil and set aside for a few hours, preferably overnight.
  • Add oil in your pan, followed by the whole spices (cumin, cardamom, cinnamon). Let them splutter then add the chopped onions and fry until slightly golden, then add the ginger and garlic pastes.
  • Fry for a minute, then add the chicken and increase the heat to medium high.
  • Fry for about 5 mins until chicken turns white, then slowly add the sour cream/yoghurt and the coconut milk.
  • Stir well and lower the heat to low, cover the pan and let these simmer for 10mins, giving a stir every now and then.
  • Check the gravy, if you feel you prefer it with a bit extra gravy, then add as much water as you need, and then mix the 1 tsp of flour/cornflour with a bit of water and add it to the dish. This will help thicken the gravy slightly.
  • Add the slit green chilli. Continue to simmer for another 10-15 mins.
  • Once the chicken is nice and tender, turn off the heat, then add the chopped coriander, salt and lemon juice.
  • If you notice, the salt is added right at the end, this helps keep the chicken very moist, flavourful and tender. If you add the salt too early, the chicken pieces will be dry and rubbery.
  • Give the chicken about 5 minutes to let the flavours infuse before serving, you can garnish with some almonds if you like. Serve with rice and naan! :)

RABRI

Mar 5th, 2013 12:00 am

The perfect creamy and rich Rabri taste divine!.And takes only 30-40 minutes to make it.The traditional way of making rabri takes more than 2 hours.So enjoy the quick and easy rabri.

Ingredients:
  • 1 tbsp Ghee
  • 15 oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 16 oz tin of Evaporated Milk
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Assorted nuts
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom powder
  • 7 to 8 Saffron strands
Method:
  • Melt down tbsp of ghee in a wide pan.After ghee is melted to that add ricotta cheese and mix well.
  • Continue to cook ricotta cheese over medium high heat for 10-15 minutes.
  • Cook till ricotta cheese comes together like a dough.
  • Now slowly add evaporated milk and as you add milk make sure you continue to stir it. Mix very well.
  • Add assorted nuts and saffron, mix well and simmer over medium high heat for 5 minutes.
  • Next add sugar, cardamom powder, mix well and cook for 5 minutes
  • Decorate with nuts and serve hot or chilled as a dessert.

DAL GOSHT(LENTIL and MEAT) CURRY

Feb 28th, 2013 12:00 am

Dal Gosht is a very tasty mutton dish cooked with lentils and masala.

Ingredients:
  • Split Bengal gram (chana dal),soaked 1/4 cup
  • Split pigeon pea (toor dal),soaked 1\8 cup
  • Split red lentil (masoor dal),soaked 1\8 cup
  • Boneless mutton,cut into 1 inch cubes
  • Oil 2 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds 1/2 tsp
  • Green chilli,slit 2
  • Ginger and Garlic paste 1/2 tbsp
  • Onion ,chopped 1 large
  • Tomato,chopped 1 medium
  • Salt to taste
  • Red chilli powder 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp
  • Garam masala powder 1/4 tsp
  • Lemon juice 1/2 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander leaves,chopped
Method:
  • Heat the oil in a pressure cooker. Add the cumin seeds and green chillies and sauté till the cumin seeds begin to change colour.
  • Add the ginger and garlic paste and saute for half a minute. Add the onion and saute till golden brown. Add the tomato and salt and continue to saute for two more minutes. Add the chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder and mutton and saute for two minutes.
  • Stir in the garam masala powder. Add one cup of water and bring to a boil. Seal the cooker with the lid and cook till the pressure is released twice (two whistles).
  • When the pressure has reduced completely, add the soaked dals and four and a half cups of water.
  • Bring to a boil, cover the pressure cooker and cook under pressure till the pressure is released eight to ten times (eight to ten whistles).
  • Once again allow the pressure to reduce. Remove the lid, stir and mash the dals lightly. Stir in the lemon juice and coriander leaves and simmer for five minutes. Serve hot.
  • Serve hot.

ALOO TIKKI(POTATO CUTLET)

Feb 25th, 2013 12:00 am

Aloo Tikki, a popular Indian snack that can be served at tea-time with the sauce or chutney of your choice.

Ingredients:
  • 3 white Potatoes
  • Cumin seeds (zeera), roasted
  • 1/4 Onion choppped finely
  • Salt to taste
  • Red chilli flakes or Green chilli
  • Coriander leaves, chopped finely
  • 1 Egg, beaten
  • Oil for shallow frying
Method:
  • Wash and quarter the potatoes.Bring a large pot of water to boil.
  • Cook potatoes in pot of boiling salted water for 10 to 15 minutes or until potatoes are tender when pierced with a fork.
  • In the meantime, place a small non stick frying pan on high heat, add cumin seeds and swirl the pan gently, in order to make sure the seeds are roasted uniformly. When you can smell the aroma and the seeds begin to darken, immediately remove the pan from the burner. Transfer to a plate to stop the cumin from cooking further.
  • When the potatoes are fork-tender, drain them, and when cool, remove the skin.
  • Mash potatoes with a masher and then with a fork, some lumps may remain, but don’t worry, as this will give a nice texture to the end product.
  • Add roasted cumin seeds, minced onion, salt, chili flakes and coriander leaves.
  • Shape into small patties.
  • Place a large non stick frying pan on medium high heat and oil for shallow frying.
  • Dip each patty into egg mixture and carefully transfer to frying pan. Fry about 40 seconds on each side,till golden.
  • Serve hot with ketchup or mint chutney.

CAULIFLOWER(GOBI) MANCHURIAN

Feb 18th, 2013 12:00 am

“Manchurian” is an Indian Chinese dish. This is one of the famous starters and it is delicious, spicy and dry or with gravy made of Cauliflower.I enjoy dry manchurian.

Ingredients:
  • 1 medium sized Cauliflower (broken into medium-sized florets)
  • Spring Onion greens chopped, for garnishing
  • Oil for deep frying
  • For Marination:
    • 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder or Green Chilli paste
    • 1/2 tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
    • Salt to taste
    • 1 small Onion, finely chopped
    • For the Batter:
      • 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour
      • 1/4 cup Corn Flour
      • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
      • 1/4 tsp Salt
      • 3/4 cup Water or as needed
    • For the Sauce:
      • 1/2 cup Sweet & Sour sauce or Tomato Ketchup
      • 1/2 tsp Red chilli or hot sauce
      • 2-3 tbsp Soya Sauce
      • 1/4 tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
      • 1 tbsp Corn flour dissolve with water
      • Salt if needed
    Method:
    • In a bowl mix together ginger and garlic paste,red chilli powder,salt and onion for the marination. Put the cauliflower florets in to the bowl and mix well and keep for about 20 minutes.
    • Meanwhile, mix together all purpose flour,corn flour,baking powder and salt in another bowl. Add the water carefully so that batter has a reasonably thick coating consistency.
    • Next drop the marinade cauliflower into the prepared batter and make sure they’re well coated with the batter.
    • Heat the oil for deep frying. Carefully drop the cauliflower into the hot oil and deep fry over medium heat, till they’re crisp and a nice brown in colour. Remove and drain on paper towels.
    • For Sauce:
      • Take medium sized pan and add sweet and sour sauce,chilli sauce,soya sauce,ginger and garlic paste
      • Heat the sauce just till it starts bubbling. Add cornflour mixture and mix well. Take the pan off the heat.
      • This sauce should be a bit on the thicker side. If you feel it is too thick, you may add a couple of spoons of water to thin it down slightly, before taking the sauce off the stove.
      • Place the fried cauliflower in a serving bowl and pour the sauce over it. Garnish with chopped spring onion greens and serve warm.

ACHARI MURGH

Feb 14th, 2013 12:00 am

Achari Murgh is a spicy and hot Chicken that is cooked in spicy North Indian style.

Ingredients:
  • Chicken-boneless or with bones
  • Onions- 1 chopped
  • Ginger and Garlic paste- 2tbsp
  • Cumin powder- 1tsp
  • Coriander powder- 2tsp
  • Red chilli powder- 3tsp
  • Salt
  • Curd -1tbsp
  • Fenugreek seeds- 1/4th tsp
  • Mustard seeds-1 tsp
  • Curry leaves
  • Dry mango powder(amchur)- 1 tsp
  • Red chilli flakes - 4-5
  • Green chillies- 4-5 (slit)
Method:
  • Take chicken and marinate it with ginger garlic paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, salt and curd.
  • Take fenugreek seeds, mustard seeds,some curry leaves(4-5), dry mango powder and chilli flakes in a grinder. Make a dry paste without water.Add to chicken and leave for one hour.
  • Take some oil( 3-4 tbsp) in a pan. Add some dried red chillies, mustard seeds, curry leaves and stir well. When done add onions and saute for 4-5 mins. Add ginger garlic paste (1tsp), coriander cumin powder-(1tsp) and saute’. Drop in 4-5 slit green chillies too.
  • Add chicken and fry for 4-5 min.
  • Now add little water enough to tenderize chicken and cover the lid.
  • Once done, garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

CHICKEN SPRING ROLLS

Feb 12th, 2013 12:00 am

Spring rolls are light easy appetizer with an exotic flavor.These are easy to make so do check out.

Spring Roll Wrapper Packet

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 pound Ground Chicken
  • 1/2 head of Small Cabbage, shredded
  • 2 Carrots, thin julienne cut
  • 1 Stalk Green Onion, chopped or Onion chopped
  • 1/2 Green,Red,Yellow Bell Peppers,thin julienne cut
  • 2 tsp Soya Sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Sesame Seeds
  • 1 tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Chilli sauce or Red chilli flakes
  • 1/4 tsp Ajinomoto-optional
  • 1 tsp Vinegar
  • Cornstarch or Cornflour-optional
  • 1 tsp All Purpose Flour or 1/2 egg white wash
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • Salt to taste
  • 50 Spring roll wrappers, defrosted
  • Oil, for deep frying
Method:
    • In a large bowl, combine the soy sauce,pepper and cornstarch. Add in the chicken and mix well. Let marinate for 10 minutes (or up to overnight in the refrigerator).
    • Heat a wok or large saute pan over high heat. When hot, add 1 tbsp of the cooking oil.Stir fry the ground chicken until browned. Remove browned ground chicken from wok to a bowl and set aside.
    • Wipe the wok clean and turn heat to medium. When just starting to get hot, add the remaining cooking oil. Add in the green onion,ginger and garlic paste and cook for 30 seconds.
    • Add in the carrots,green bell pepper,red bell pepper,yellow bell pepper and cabbage. Stir well and turn the heat to medium-high. Stir fry the vegetables for 2 minutes,or until the veggies have softened.
    • Add the cooked ground chicken back into the wok, stir well. Add in the soya sauce,chilli sauce or red chilli flakes,sesame seeds,vinegar,ajinomotto and toss again.Cook until the liquid dries off.
    • In a small bowl,add water and all purpose flour and make a smooth paste. Open the spring roll sheet package, cover with barely damp towel to prevent drying out.
    • Add 1 tablespoon of filling to spring roll and roll up. Secure the edge with all purpose flour paste. Keep rolled spring rolls covered with plastic wrap or in ziploc bag to prevent drying.
    •  To fry, heat oil in a wok or deep.Carefully slide in the spring rolls, one at a time, to the oil to fry. Turn the spring rolls to brown evenly and fry for about 3 minutes. Let cool on paper towel.
    • Serve hot with ketchup or Chinese hot sauce.

Enjoy :)

EGG BIRYANI

Feb 8th, 2013 12:00 am

Egg Biryani, absolutely delicious recipe! A simple to make super recipe of Egg Biryani, that your family will love. An easy and great dish for egg lovers.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 tsp Biryani Masala powder
  • Ghee
  • 6 eggs
  • Water
  • 1 tbsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 4 Green Chillies
  • Oil
  • 1 Onion large
  • 2 cup Plain Yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices-cardamon,cloves,cinnamon stick,bay leaves and shazeera
  • Fresh Coriander/Cilantro Leaves and Mint leaves-finely chopped
Method:
  • Wash and soak rice for atleast ½ hour.
  • Next boil the eggs and then shell the boiled eggs and give four long cuts on the white portion of each egg without separating it.
  • In a khadhai or pot, heat the ghee or oil.Add whole garam masala spices and onions,fry the onions until they become golden brown and then add ginger and garlic paste ,green chillies ,mint leaves,coriander leaves,biryani masala and curd and cook.
  • And then add 1 1/2 times the amount of water to rice and bring it to boil.
  • Next add the soaked rice and mix well and cook covered till rice is done.
  • Meanwhile take a small bowl add red chilli powder,salt,gram flour(besan), 1 beaten egg and mix well.Next Dip each boiled egg into the mixture and keep aside.
  • Take a pan add butter and fry the eggs for few mins and then remove and keep aside.
  • Now check the rice,when the air holes appear on the rice surface, reduce the stove heat to low. Cook until all of the water has absorbed and the rice is tender.
  • When the rice is cooked top it with eggs.
  • Serve hot.
