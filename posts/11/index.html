VANILLA SPONGE CAKE

Sep 15th, 2012 12:00 am

I baked this cake for my lovely sis’s back there in India.It really turned out too good and spongy..

Ingredients:
  • Maida 2 cups
  • Eggs 2
  • Sugar 60gms
  • Vanilla Essence 1tsp
Method:
    • Firstly separate egg yolk and egg white into two separate bowls.
    • Now beat egg white with beater or whisker.
    • Take about 60 gms of sugar and add it to egg white and beat it until sugar dissolves.
    • Preheat oven to 180’c
    • Next add the egg yolk and beat it and also add 1tsp vanilla essece .
    • Now add 2 cups maida and beat well so to form the cake batter.
    • Take spatulla and mix it lightly.
    • Finally take a cake mould,spread the butter on all sides and place the batter and tap it to level.
    • Bake for about 20mins and Set for 5 mins.
    • Then cut into slices and serve with ice-cream,fruits or cherries.

Enjoy :)

 

FRIED MEAT

Sep 13th, 2012 12:00 am

This is a slightly spicy dry side-dish.Extremely simple,it tastes great when paired with pulao.

Ingredients:
  • Meat
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 2-3 Green chillies
  • 1tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala powder
  • Curry leaves
  • Lemon juice
Method:
  • Clean and cut the meat into even sized cubes.
  • Marinate meat with ginger-garlic paste,salt,turmeric powder,red chilli powder,coriander powder and garam masala.Keep aside atleast for half an hour.
  • Saute the onions till golden brown and then add curry leaves and green chillies.
  • Next add the marinated meat and half a cup of water.Cover and cook on low heat till the meat is tender.
  • Once the meat is well cooked, stir-fry continuously till it turns dry and dark brown in colour.
  • Once done add lemon juice and serve with onions or capsicum.

TOMATO CHICKEN

Sep 4th, 2012 12:00 am

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 kg Bone/Boneless Chicken
  • 1 Onion -finely chopped
  • 2 Tomatoes-finely chopped
  • 1tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1tbsp Red chilli powder
  • 1/4tbsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 Garam masala powder
  • 2 Green chillies
  • 1tbsp lemon juice-optional
  • Salt to taste
  • 4tbsp oil
  • Coriander leaves-finely chopped
  • Mint leaves-finely chopped
  • Fenugreek leaves(Methi)-finely chopped-optional
Method:
  • In a large non-stick saucepan,add oil at medium heat and add onion;fry till they are slightly golden in color.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste,fry for 1min and then add tomatoes,fenugreek leaves,red chilli powder,turmeric powder,green chilli and mix well.
  • Stir till the tomatoes gets smooth and mushy.
  • Next clean and wash the chicken pieces,add it garam masala powder and mix well,without adding too much of water,until the chicken is tender.
  • Then leave it for low heat till the thin layer of oil appears on the bottom surface and cover the pan and leave till the gravy is thick.
  • Stir every 10 mins and add salt to taste.Do not cook on high heat.
  • Add coriander and mint leaves and cover.
  • Remove from heat and serve with parathas and plain rice.

POTATO and CHEESE CROQUETTES

Sep 1st, 2012 12:00 am

Potato and cheese croquettes are golden and crispy on the outside and cheesy and creamy on the inside,which can be served as a starter or simply as an evening snack.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2kg Potatoes
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 1green chillies finely chopped
  • 2tbsp green coriander fine chop
  • 1tsp Black pepper
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp crushed red chilli-optional
  • 3tbsp cheddar cheese-grated
  • oil for frying
  • For Coating:
    • Cornflour or Maida
    • 1 egg beaten
    • 1 cup bread crumbs
Method:
    • Firstly peel,boil and mash the potatoes.
    • Now mix potatoes,spices and chopped onion,green coriander,cheese and green chilies till combined.
    • Make any shape you like;round or cylindrical shape.
    • Next coat croquettes in corn flour then in egg and in bread crumbs.
    • Take a pan,add oil.Let the oil heat and then fry the croquettes on high heat till golden brown.
    • Serve the croquettes wuth ketchup .

Enjoy it with any drink:)

HUMMUS

Aug 23rd, 2012 12:00 am

Hummus is one of the most popular Middle Eastern dips,served with pita bread. This dish always reminds me of those days when I use to have with my family during weekends in Saudi. So do try this recipe.

Enjoy falafel chips dipped in hummus.Have a bite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 16oz can of chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup liquid from can of chickpeas
  • 3-5 tbsp lemon juice(depending on taste)
  • 1 1/2 tsp tahini -you can find in arab or indian grocery stores
  • 2 cloves garlic,crushed
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2tbsp olive oil

Method:

  • Drain the chickpeas and set aside liquid from can.
  • Combine remaining ingredients in blender or food processor.Add 1/4 cup of liquid from chickpeas.
  • Blend for 3-5 minutes on low until thoroughly mixed and smooth.
  • Place in serving bowl;and create a shallow well in the center of the hummus.
  • Add a small amount around 1-2 tbsp olive oil in the well.
  • For a spicer hummus add a dash of cayenne pepper and garnish with chickpeas or parsley.
  • Serve immediately with fresh,warm pita bread.

Storing Hummus

Hummus can be refrigerated for upto 3 days and can be kept in the freezer for 1 month.Add a little olive oil if it appears too dry.

CHICKEN FRIED RICE

Aug 19th, 2012 12:00 am

Ingredients:
  • Boneless Chicken
  • Rice 95%cooked
  • Black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Cornflour
  • Eggs-2
  • Chopped Garlic
  • Chopped Onions
  • Ajinomoto
  • Veggies-Chopped Carrots,beans and muttor or can use frozen veggies
  • Soya sauce
  • Chilli garlic sauce
  • Chopped Spring onion
Method:
  • Cut boneless chicken into small pieces.Sprinkle little pepper,salt,cornflour and add 2 drops of egg.
  • Cook rice by draining .After you boil;drain away the water and spread rice on tray.
  • Next take pan,add oil and fry the chicken.
  • Next drain the oil keep only few drops of oil.Now add finely chopped garlic and saute for 10 minutes.
  • Add chopped onions and saute till transparent.Pour 2 beaten eggs and cook.
  • Next add pepper,salt,ajinomoto,chopped veggies,few drops of vinegar and soya sauce.
  • Now add the fried chicken and also the rice in batches and mix well.Remember rice should be 95% cooked and should be fluffy.
  • Add little salt,soya sauce on top.Add chilli garlic sauce and cook for few mins.
  • Garnish with chopped spring onions.

CAPSICUM CHICKEN

Aug 10th, 2012 12:00 am

This recipe is a combination of chicken and capsicums that tastes too good.

Ingredients:
  • 1 chicken cut into 8 pcs or half a kilo of boneless chicken breast cubed
  • 2 green capsicums
  • small bunch of coriander
  • 2 green chillies
  • 300 ml youghurt (or more if you want extra gravy)
  • 1tbsp tomato paste
  • salt to taste
  • 1tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1tbsp oil
  • pinch of turmeric
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
Method:
  • First cut the capsicum and add it with yoghurt,coriander and green chillies into a blender and blend until smooth.
  • Pour this mixture over the chicken and all the other ingredients tomato paste,salt ,ginger-garlic paste,turmeric and garam masala into the chicken.
  • Take a pan or cooker, add 1tbsp oil and the chicken into it and cook.
  • Simmer gently till the chicken is soft ,add coriander leaves and water if necessary (depends on the preference in consistency) and cook for few mins.
  • Remove and Serve with naan or rice :)

RUSK RABDI

Aug 5th, 2012 12:00 am

This is one of the other forms of rabdi. This was the first dish I prepared at my in-laws place;which was loved by all. 

Ingredients:
  • Milk 1L
  • Condensed Milk/Milkmaid 1/2cup
  • Cake Rusk
  • Vanilla custard powder 1tbsp
  • Sugar
  • For Decoration-strawberries,blueberries ,cherries or any fruits.
Method:
  • Layer the cake rusk in flat dish and sprinkle little milk on the rusk or you can dip each rusk in milk to keep it moist.
  • Make Custard
    • Take a saucepan;add milk and bring to boil and then add sugar.
    • Add condensed milk or milkmaid.
    • Next in a small cup add custard powder and little milk and stir continuously to avoid lumps.
    • Add this custard to the boiling milk and stir well on high flame.Cook until it becomes a little thick in consistency.
    • Remove from stove and let it cool down.
  • Next add the custard on to the layered cake rusk.
  • Decorate the dessert with strawberries,blueberries or any fruits.
  • Refrigerate and Serve.

Enjoy :)
