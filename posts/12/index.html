CARAMEL PUDDING

Nov 11th, 2012 12:00 am

I made these delightful puddings from a ready made carton mix today as wanted to prepare something quick.This makes a beautiful and quick dessert. This dessert always remind me of my sweet little cousin sis who calls me by the name Pudding di :).It’s kind of long story why she kept my name pudding..lol

This is how the pudding packet looks like..

Ingredients:
  • Milk 2cups
  • Caramel pudding powder packet
  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • Cardamom pods
Method:
  • Boil the milk (as per the carton instructions),while boiling add a stick of cinnamon and a couple of cardamom pods for better taste.
  • Empty the caramel sauce (that comes in the box) in a dish and keep aside.
  • Once the milk reaches near boiling point,then add the pudding powder that comes in the box.And then pour into the caramel coated molds/dish.
  • Make sure you discard the cinnamon stick and cardamom pods first though! Serve chilled! )
  • Cool and refrigerate for few hours atleast 1hour before serving.

POTATO MUTTOR CURRY

Nov 8th, 2012 12:00 am

If you’re in a rush but want something tasty and wholesome to eat, this is the dish for you. Potato muttor curry is a great lunch or dinner dish and is perfect with freshly made puris or fried Indian bread.

Ingredients:
  • 2tbsps Oil
  • 1tsp Cumin seeds
  • 2 Green chillies slit
  • 1 Onion diced
  • 1tsp Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 2 Tomatoes diced
  • 2tsps Coriander powder
  • 1tsp Cumin powder
  • 1/2tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1tsp Dry mango powder-optional
  • 1tsp Garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 5-6 Potatoes- peeled and cut into cubes
  • 1 cup Peas -(you can use frozen peas as well)
  • Coriander leaves-finely chopped to garnish
Method:
  • In a wok/pressure cooker heat the oil and add the cumin seeds to it when hot.Once they sizzle and turn slightly darker add the green chillies and then the onions.
  • Saute the onions till they turn soft.Add the ginger and garlic pastes and saute for another minute.
  • Add the tomatoes, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric, red chilli and raw mango powders and saute for a minute.
  • Last of all add the potatoes and peas and half a cup of water.Add salt to taste.
  • Cover the pan and cook till the potatoes are soft.
  • When the potatoes are cooked, turn off the stove,sprinkle the garam masala over the dish and cover immediately.
  • In a few minutes open, garnish with the finely chopped coriander leaves and serve.

VEGGIE CUTLET

Nov 5th, 2012 12:00 am

A healthy,tasty and nutritious cutlet These easy vegetarian patties are great for a snack or as an appetizer. Serve with ketchup or Indian style mint chutney on the side.

Ingredients:
  • Potatoes -2 medium
  • Mixed vegetables (green peas, carrots, green beans) 1cup chopped
  • Onion 1 chopped fine
  • Coriander-finely chopped
  • Green Chillies – to taste, finely chopped
  • Chopped ginger 1 teaspoon
  • Salt (adjust to your taste)
  • Red chilli powder 1/2 tsp(adjust according to heat level desired)
  • Coriander powder 1/4 tsp
  • Cumin powder 1/4tsp
  • Oil to fry
  • For Coating:
    • 3 tbsp maida or all purpose flour mixed into a paste with 3 tbsp water (approx.)
    • 2 slices of bread, toasted dry on a skilled and powdered or 1 cup breadcrumbs
Method:
  • Boiled the potatoes until they are tender.Once cooked, drain the water and let the potatoes cool down.Peel the skin off and mash the potatoes.
  • Steam the chopped mixed vegetables.Make sure to squeeze the vegetables to take the excess water out and Pat dry.
  • Mix all the ingredients togather; adjust salt and pepper to your taste.
  • Add 4 tablespoons of water to the All Purpose flour to make a batter and keep aside.
  • With oiled hands, divide the mixture into equal parts.Shape into flat round patties about 1/2 inch thick.
  • Place each patty in the flour-water mixture and coat well. Then place on the bread crumbs and coat well.
  • Heat the oil on medium high heat in a frying pan. Frying pan should have atleast 1 1/2 inch of oil. To check if the oil is ready, put one drop of batter in the oil, the batter should come up right away.
  • Shallow fry until golden brown on both sides. Serve hot with ketchup.

TOMATO PULAO

Nov 1st, 2012 12:00 am

Tomato Pulao is a tasty tangy rice dish that is just delicious and taste great when paired with raita.

Ingredients:
  • 2cups basmati rice
  • 3cups Water
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 2 tomatoes chopped with the peel on
  • 1/2cup tomato paste
  • 1tbsp yoghurt
  • 1/2tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp Shahzeera
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 sticks of Cinnamon
  • 2 Cloves
  • 2 Cardamons
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander-finely chopped
  • 2tbsp ghee
  • 2tbsp oil
Method:
  • Wash and soak the rice for half an hour.
  • Take a pan and add the oil and ghee .Then add the spices;shahzeera,cinnamon sticks,cloves,cardamons and pepper.
  • Once they splutter in the oil,add chopped onions and fry them till they are translucent.Then add turmeric and coriander powder.
  • Then add ginger-garlic paste,chopped tomato,tomato paste and yoghurt.
  • Cook until the mixture thickens and add water and salt.As soon as the water boils,add rice by draining the soaked water.
  • Sprinkle a few coriander leaves and let it cook on medium flame by stiring now and then.
  • When you find very little water left,reduce the heat and cook with lid on.
  • Once the rice is cooked.Serve hot.

FRUIT TRIFLE-Eid Special

Oct 26th, 2012 12:00 am

Wishing you all a very Happy Eid!!!Hope you have a fun day with family & friends.Here is Eid special Dessert for all my lovely people:)

Fruit Trifle is a fantastic dessert any time of year, but especially when the fruit ingredients are in season.This is the easy and tasty dessert.

Ingredients:
  • Milk 1l
  • Condensed Milk/Milkmaid 1/2cup
  • Chocolate cake
  • Vanilla custard powder 1tbsp
  • Sugar as taste
  • Fruits-pineapple chunks,apple,strawberries,blueberries ,cherries or any fruits.
Method:
  • Make Custard
    • Take a saucepan;add milk and bring to boil and then add sugar.
    • Add condensed milk or milkmaid.
    • Next in a small cup add custard powder and little milk and stir continuously to avoid lumps.
    • Add this custard to the boilng milk and stir well on high flame.Cook until it becomes a little thick in consistency.
    • Remove from stove and let it cool down.
  • In a deep, clear glass dish or trifle bowl, spread a layer of chocolate cake chunks.You can spinkle a little orange juice on the cake to keep it moist
  • Scatter the cake with chopped strawberries and apples,pineapple chunks or any fruit of your choice. And then pour the custard thus forming the first layer.
  • Repeat again by layering the cake and then scatter some fruits and then pour custard.
  • Decorate the dessert with strawberries,blueberries or any fruits.
  • Refrigerate for one hour and Serve.

Enjoy this festive dessert with your family and friends:)

DAHI KI KHADI

Oct 25th, 2012 12:00 am

Dahi KI Khadi with plain rice is a delicious Indian recipe served as a curry.

Ingredients:

  • Ingredients for Kadi
    • 2 cups Yogurt
    • 1 Onion-chopped
    • 4-6 Green chilies
    • 1/4 tbsp Turmeric powder
    • 1 tbsp Ginger paste & Garlic paste
    • Salt to taste
    • 3 tbsp Gram Flour(Besan)
    • 5-6 Curry leaves
    • 2 - 4 Garlic Cloves
  • For seasoning (Baghar)
    • 4 tbsp Oil
    • 1 tbsp Cumin seeds (Zeera)
    • 1 tbsp Black mustard seeds (Rai)
    • 2-4 Garlic Cloves
    • 2 Dried Red chillies
    • Few Curry leaves
  • Ingredients for Pakoda(Fritters)
    • 1 cup Gram Flour(Besan)
    • 1 tbsp Red Chili powder
    • 1/4 tbsp Turmeric powder
    • 1/2 tbsp Baking Soda
    • 1 tbsp Ginger paste & Garlic paste
    • Coriander leaves-finely chopped
    • Mint leaves-finely chopped
    • 1/2 tsp Coriander powder-optional
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for frying

Method:

  • Make Pakodi(Fritters):
    • In a bowl add gram flour, salt,ginger and garlic paste, red chili powder,turmeric powder,coriander powder and baking soda. Mix well and make a smooth paste by adding water gradually.
    • Heat oil in a frying pan for frying pakodas. Use your hands or spoon to pick a small amount of pakoda mixture and drop it in hot oil.
    • Fry them on medium flame until they turn golden brown in color from all sides. Take them out once they are ready and switch off the stove. You can keep them on tissue paper to drain excess oil.
    • Keep them aside.
  • Make Kadi:
    • In a large bowl add the yoghurt; beat it with the spoon or a whisk to make it smooth.
    • Then in a small cup add water mix gram flour without lumps.Pour over into the yoghurt mix it well without lumps
    • Add ginger & garlic paste, turmeric powder, salt, and mix it well.
    • In a large cooking vessel pour yoghurt mixture over a tea net to trap all of the loose mixture.
    • Then add sliced onion, green chillies,curry leaves and garlic cloves and leave it on for low heat till its thick paste.
    • Stir often to prevent burning, if required, add little water/gram from time to time prevent from burning.
    • Once cooked drop the pakodas into it.
    • Now it’s time to add the final punch by adding the tadka.You need to add this just before serving.
    • Heat oil in a small frying pan on medium flame. Once it is hot (in approx 30 seconds), add cumin seeds, black mustard seeds, garlic cloves, dried red chlilies and then switch off the stove.
    • Immediately add this tadka to your khadi and mix well.
    • Your delicious Khadi Pakoda is ready to enjoy with Rice or with Naan/Chapati/Paratha.

CHICKEN CURRY

Oct 21st, 2012 12:00 am

An easy Chicken creamy curry must must try it.

Ingredients:
  • Chicken 1kg
  • Oil 1/2cup
  • Onion-1 medium finely chopped
  • Ginger and Garlic paste 1tbsp
  • Yogurt 1cup
  • Red chilli powder 1tbsp
  • Coriander powder 1tsp
  • Garam masala powder 1/2tsp
  • Dried fenugreek leaves 1tsp
  • Heavy cream 2tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Green chillies 2-3
  • Coriandar leaves-finely chopped
Method:
  • Wash and marinate the chicken with ginger and garlic paste,salt,red chilli powder,garam masala powder,coriander powder,yogurt,and fenugreek leaves.
  • Heat the oil and saute the chopped onions for 2-3minutes.
  • Add the chicken and mix well.Let it cook for few minutes.
  • Next pour a glass of water and cook on medium heat until all the water dries up and it forms a thick gravy.
  • Add cream, mix and cook well for two minutes and garnish with green chilli rings and coriander leaves.

OREO MILKSHAKE

Oct 19th, 2012 12:00 am

No matter how you put Oreo Cookies and ice cream together,you know it’s going to be good.This cool,creamy and chocolaty treat is case in point:)

Ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup cold milk
  • 1/2 cup chocolate syrup
  • 2 cups Vanilla ice-cream
  • 14 Oreo Cookies
Method:
  • Crush 14 of the cookies coarsely;place in blend container.
  • Add milk,vanilla ice cream and chocolate syrup.
  • Cover and blend until smooth.
  • Pour into glass.Top it with scoop of ice cream and a cookie.
  • Serve immediately.

DREAMWHIP FRUIT TRIFLE

Oct 15th, 2012 12:00 am

A light, fresh, and cool dessert.Easy Recipe,Perfect for Parties.

Here is the image of the Dreamwhip whipped topping mix packet

Ingredients:
  • 2 Envelopes Dream whip Whipped topping mix
  • 1 tin Nestle cream
  • 1 tin Fruit Cocktail or fresh fruits of your choice
  • 10 Bread slices
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1 cup Water
Method:
  • Beat the dream whip whipped topping mix and nestle cream and set aside.
  • Prepare the Sugar syrup-Take a saucepan and boil the water and sugar until the sugar dissolves completely and keep aside.
  • Take 10 slices of bread and cut all the brown sides of bread and cut into small cubes.
  • Now dip each bread cube into the sugar syrup and place it in the flat glass dish and then pour a layer of beaten dreamwhip on bread and then put some fruit cocktail.
  • Next form the second layer by placing the bread cubes dipped in sugar syrup ,then pour the dreamwhip and then top it with fruit cocktails.
  • Refrigerate for 4-5 hours and Serve chilled.
  • Enjoy the dessert:)

KHEEMA

Oct 11th, 2012 12:00 am

Kheema or mince can be made with any meat you prefer. It is extremely versatile.Kheema tastes delicious with Chapatis or Parathas.

Ingredients:
  • Minced Meat 1/2 kg
  • Potatoes 2,cubed
  • Onions 2, large (thinly sliced)
  • Tomatoes 3, medium (roughly sliced)
  • Ginger and Garlic Paste: 1 1/2 tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Red Chilli Powder 1tbsp  or as to taste
  • Turmeric Powder 1tsp
  • Coriander Powder 1 1/2 tsp
  • Tomato Ketchup 2 tbsp
  • Green Cardamom 3-4
  • Cloves 4
  • Green Chillies 3-4, pricked
  • Coriander Leaves 1/2 bunch, finely chopped
  • Oil: 1 cup
Method:
  • In a deep cooking pan, heat oil and fry thinly sliced onion till it gets soft and transparent.
  • Stir in the cloves, green cardamoms along with ginger and ginger pastes. Now fry all till onions turn light golden brown.
  • Add in minced meat, salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder and coriander powder. Stir fry till minced meat loses it’s pink color. Add enough water that covers minced meat. Put on medium heat and cook till minced meat is half done.
  • Now add potatoes and tomatoes. If required, then add a little more water so that potatoes gets cooked. Stir, taste and fix salt at this point. Cover and cook till potatoes are almost done.
  • Now add in tomato ketchup and green chillies. Stir and cover on medium heat so that water is well absorbed and potatoes are nicely cooked. Lastly, stir in the coriander leaves and switch off the flame.
  • Serve hot with Paratha or Chapati or Naan.
