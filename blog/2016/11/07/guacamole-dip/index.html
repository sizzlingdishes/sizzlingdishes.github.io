GUACAMOLE DIP-FAMOUS MEXICAN DIP

Nov 7th, 2016 12:39 pm

Guacamole is a healthy and delicious snack enjoyed with tortilla chips,crackers or any chips. It can be served along with lamb chops as well.Do make this healthy homemade dip.

Ingredients:
  • 2 Ripe avocados
  • 2 Onions medium-finely chopped
  • 1 cup Tomatoes-chopped
  • 1 tsp Red chilli sauce
  • Coriander leaves- chopped
  • Jalapeno -seeded and chopped(optional)
  • 1/2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • Garlic 4-5 cloves-crushed
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder or to taste
  • Salt to taste
Method:
  • Cut the avocado into half and discard the seed. Scoop out flesh using a spoon.
  • Using a fork mash well.Blend together avocado, onions, garlic cloves, lemon juice, salt, black peppercorns and jalapeno to a fine paste.
  • Serve with tortilla chips or serve as a side dish with lamb chops.
